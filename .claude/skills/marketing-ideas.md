# Skill: Marketing Ideas

## Quick Win Campaign Ideas

### 1. "Neighborhood Discount" Campaign
When we're already in a neighborhood for one job, offer a discount to homes on the same street.
- Post in neighborhood Facebook groups: "We're in [Neighborhood] this week — if 3 homes book, everyone saves 15%"
- Danny can pull addresses, Jasmine posts the Facebook message
- Low cost, high ROI (already driving the truck there)

### 2. "Before You List" Push with Realtors
Carla targets listing agents, offers them a fast-turn service for pre-listing cleanups.
- Position as a listing prep service, not just cleaning
- Before photos → after photos → realtor shares on their social
- Refer their listings back to them when customers ask about buying/selling

### 3. Annual Plan Upsell After First Job
Every new customer gets a follow-up email 3 days after the job:
- "How did it turn out? Here's what we recommend for maintenance..."
- Offer the annual plan at the time they're happiest (right after a great job)
- Nina tracks this in HubSpot automatically

### 4. Google Review Campaign
Ask for reviews immediately after the job while the customer is still happy:
- Text-based ask: "Would you mind leaving us a Google review? It means a lot to a local business."
- Direct link to the Google review page
- Track review count as a weekly KPI
- More reviews → higher ranking in local search → free leads

### 5. Before/After Photo Contest
- "Send us your before photos — worst exterior wins a free cleaning"
- Generates content, engagement, and lead list all at once
- Jasmine runs it on Facebook

### 6. "Thank Your Neighbor" Referral
After every job, text the customer: "If any neighbors ask who cleaned your house, here's a $25 off code for them."
- Direct-neighbor referrals are the highest-converting source for home services
- Automate via Zapier + HubSpot

## Seasonal Content Ideas by Month

**January:** "Spring booking opens soon — here's what to expect"
**February:** "Spring is 6 weeks away. Here's what Ohio winter does to your siding."
**March:** "Algae is back. Here's what this Cuyahoga County driveway looked like before we arrived."
**April:** "Spring clean is in full swing — before/after from this week's jobs"
**May:** "Pre-Memorial Day clean — get it done before the cookouts"
**June:** "Property managers: mid-year exterior maintenance"
**July:** "Hottest month = most algae growth. Here's why."
**August:** "Fall is 6 weeks away. Book your pre-winter clean."
**September:** "Fall clean is the move. Road salt season is coming."
**October:** "Last chance before temperatures drop"
**November:** "Annual plan — lock in spring + fall pricing before year-end"
**December:** "Gift cards available. Give the gift of curb appeal."
