## Forest City in One Paragraph
Forest City Power Washing is a residential and commercial exterior cleaning company in Cleveland, Ohio. Bradley is the AGM and works in the field. Service area: 7 counties — Cuyahoga, Lake, Lorain, Geauga, Medina, Summit, Portage. Sister company to Fly By Junk Haulers. Recently rebranded from CLEan Power Washing.

## Services
House washing (soft wash) · Roof soft washing · Driveway/concrete cleaning · Deck/fence cleaning · Commercial building washing

## Packages
- **Fresh Start** — entry level, single surface or basic whole-home
- **Curb Appeal** — full exterior, most popular
- **Showstopper** — premium, everything + roof wash
- **Annual Plan** — spring + fall visit, recurring revenue

## ICPs
- Residential: homeowners 35–65, Northeast Ohio suburbs, care about curb appeal
- Commercial: property managers, HOA companies, facility managers
- Partners: siding contractors, landscapers, realtors, gutter companies, painters

## Top 3 Objections
1. "Cheaper on Facebook" → not the cheapest, but we show up and do it right
2. "I'll rent a pressure washer" → safe for concrete, NOT safe for siding/roof (warranty void, damage)
3. "Don't need it often" → 12–18 month algae cycle in Northeast Ohio climate

## Voice
Sound like Bradley: direct, friendly, Cleveland local. No corporate speak. Short sentences. Active voice. Reference Northeast Ohio, specific counties.

## Don't Say
"Leverage" · "Synergize" · "World-class" · "Best-in-class" · "Our team of professionals" · "We are committed to"
