# Skill: Customer Research

## Voice of Customer (VOC) Mining

The goal: find the exact words customers use when they talk about exterior cleaning. These words go directly into copy, ads, and email sequences.

## Where to Mine Reviews

1. **Google Maps** — search "[competitor name] power washing" in Cleveland/Northeast Ohio area. Read 1-star, 3-star, and 5-star reviews.
2. **Yelp** — same search. Yelp often has more detailed reviews.
3. **Facebook reviews** — search competitor Facebook pages
4. **HomeAdvisor / Angi** — detailed project reviews with customer context
5. **Nextdoor** — search "power washing" in Northeast Ohio neighborhoods for organic recommendations and complaints

## What to Extract

### From 5-star reviews (what went right):
- What specifically did they compliment? (showed up on time, friendly, thorough, etc.)
- What outcome did they describe? ("looked like a brand new house", "roof is white again")
- What were they worried about before booking? (often mentioned)
- What would they tell a friend?

### From 3-star reviews (what was missing):
- What unmet expectation? (communication, result, price)
- This reveals what customers assume but vendors miss

### From 1-star reviews (what went wrong):
- No-shows, damage, hidden fees, upselling pressure
- These are Forest City's differentiation opportunities — build them into messaging

## VOC Output Format

Organize into a table:

| Exact customer phrase | Theme | Emotion | Use In |
|----------------------|-------|---------|--------|
| "looked green and slimy" | Problem description | Disgust | Headlines, Facebook posts |
| "like a brand new house" | Outcome | Pride | Package descriptions, ads |
| "they actually showed up" | Reliability | Relief | Differentiator copy |
| "didn't know soft washing was a thing" | Education | Curiosity | Educational content |

## Market Research Framework

When analyzing the Northeast Ohio market:

1. **Seasonal demand drivers**
   - Lake Erie humidity = heavy algae growth spring/summer
   - Ohio winters = road salt staining on concrete/lower siding
   - Hot humid summers = mold and mildew on north-facing surfaces

2. **Homeowner segments**
   - Pre-sale prep (highest urgency, budget-flexible)
   - Annual maintenance (recurring revenue)
   - Post-winter first-timer (just noticed it, lower urgency)
   - Commercial maintenance (multi-property, value contract)

3. **Competitor landscape**
   - Watch for: are they positioning on price, reliability, or quality?
   - What do their negative reviews say? (That's the gap Forest City fills)
   - Are they soft wash specialists or just pressure washers? (specialization matters for roofs)
