# Skill: Launch Strategy

## County Expansion Framework

When Forest City is ready to push hard into a new county, run a 60-day launch sprint.

### Pre-Launch (2 weeks before)
- [ ] Danny pulls 30 commercial prospects in target county (property managers, facility managers)
- [ ] Carla identifies 10 contractor partners (siding, landscaping, gutters) in that county
- [ ] Marcus profiles top 3 competitors operating in that county
- [ ] Rick prepares Facebook ad set targeted to that county's homeowners
- [ ] Jasmine writes 5 county-specific social posts

### Week 1–2: Outreach Blitz
- Danny sends first cold email to all 30 commercial prospects
- Carla sends referral partner outreach to 10 contractors
- Facebook ads go live targeting county zip codes
- Bradley posts in local Facebook community groups (introduce Forest City)

### Week 3–4: Follow-Up and First Jobs
- Danny sends Email 2 in sequence
- Carla follows up with any contractor replies
- Run a county-specific intro offer if needed: "$50 off first job in [County]"
- Document first jobs in that county — get before/after photos for social

### Week 5–8: Build the Base
- Danny's Email 3 (break-up) and triage replies
- Carla adds confirmed partners to HubSpot with quarterly check-in reminders
- Jasmine posts a county-specific before/after
- Report on: jobs completed, revenue, partners added, CPA from ads

## Seasonal Campaign Framework

### Spring Blitz (Start January, Run Feb–April)
**Goal:** Book out April and May before peak season
**Message:** Algae is coming back. Book before we're full.
**Channels:** Facebook ads, email to past customers, Danny outbound to commercial
**Assets needed:** 3 Facebook ad sets, email to past customer list, 5 social posts

### Summer Maintenance Push (June–July)
**Goal:** Fill gaps in the schedule with commercial and repeat residential
**Message:** Property managers — your building needs a mid-year wash
**Channels:** Danny outbound to commercial, Carla partner check-ins, LinkedIn

### Fall Rush (August–October)
**Goal:** Second biggest season. Pre-winter clean.
**Message:** Get it done before temperatures drop. Book by [date].
**Channels:** Facebook retargeting, email to spring customers, social posts

### Annual Plan Campaign (October–November)
**Goal:** Convert one-time customers to recurring
**Message:** Lock in spring + fall pricing now. Set it and forget it.
**Channels:** Email to closed-won customers, social, estimates follow-ups
