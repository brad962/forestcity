# Skill: Ad Creative

## Facebook Ad Structure

Every Facebook ad has three jobs: stop the scroll, make them care, make them act.

### Hook Formulas (first line)
- **Fear/Risk:** "Your roof has algae eating into the shingles right now. Here's how to stop it."
- **Before/After tension:** "This is what a Northeast Ohio driveway looks like after one Ohio winter."
- **Local specificity:** "Cuyahoga County homeowners: spring cleaning season is 2 weeks away."
- **Question:** "When's the last time your siding was actually clean?"
- **Bold claim:** "We washed 500 Northeast Ohio homes last year. Zero damage. Zero no-shows."
- **Curiosity:** "Most people don't know what those black streaks on their roof actually are."

### Ad Body (4–6 sentences)
1. Expand on the hook with the real problem
2. Agitate: what happens if they don't act
3. Introduce the solution (Forest City) — one sentence
4. One proof point (years in business, reviews, # homes cleaned)
5. Call to action

### CTAs for Facebook
- "Get a free quote — takes 2 minutes."
- "Book before [month] and save 10%."
- "DM us your address for an instant estimate."
- "Click to see our before/after gallery."
- "Tap 'Learn More' — we serve [county] and 6 surrounding counties."

## Ad Variations to Write Per Batch

Always write 5–10 variations per campaign, testing:
- 3 different hooks
- 2 different CTAs
- 2 different angles (fear vs. aspiration)

## Before/After Carousel Ad
- Slide 1: Before photo with headline ("Your home could look like this")
- Slide 2: During / process shot ("2 hours later…")
- Slide 3: After photo ("This is the same house")
- Slide 4: CTA card ("Free quote for Northeast Ohio homeowners")

## Google Ad Structure

**Headline 1 (30 chars):** Location + service
Examples: "Cleveland Power Washing", "Northeast Ohio Soft Wash"

**Headline 2 (30 chars):** Trust signal or offer
Examples: "Free Quotes · 7 Counties", "Insured · No-Show Guarantee"

**Headline 3 (30 chars):** CTA
Examples: "Book Online Today", "Call for Spring Pricing"

**Description 1 (90 chars):**
"House washing, roof soft wash, driveway cleaning. We serve Cuyahoga, Lake, Lorain + 4 more counties."

**Description 2 (90 chars):**
"Locally owned and operated in Cleveland. Fully insured. 500+ homes cleaned. Get a free estimate today."

## Targeting Notes
- Facebook: homeowners 35–65, Northeast Ohio zip codes, interests: home improvement, real estate
- Google: location-targeted to service area, search intent keywords only
- Retargeting: anyone who visited the website or watched 50%+ of a video ad
