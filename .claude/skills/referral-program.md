# Skill: Referral Program

## The Program Design

### What We Offer Partners
- **Cash referral fee:** $50 per closed job from their referral
- **Reciprocal referrals:** We refer their services to our residential customers
- **Easy process:** One text or email to connect the lead — no paperwork
- **No exclusivity required:** They can still refer others

### What Partners Get in Return (from our side)
Forest City touches a homeowner's exterior every cleaning. They're in position to refer:
- Siding repair/replacement
- Painting (post-clean is a great time to paint)
- Landscaping updates
- Gutter repair/replacement
- Roof repairs (we see roof condition on every wash)

### Partner Onboarding Process
1. First outreach (cold email or LinkedIn)
2. If reply → 10-minute phone call to explain the arrangement
3. Add to HubSpot as "Referral Partner" contact type
4. Send them 5 Forest City business cards (physical)
5. Tag in HubSpot for quarterly check-in

## Outreach Message Frameworks

### Cold Email to Contractor
Subject: "Siding contractors + power washing — quick question"

"Hey [Name], I'm Bradley with Forest City Power Washing. We wash houses across Northeast Ohio — Cuyahoga, Lake, Lorain, and the surrounding counties.

I reach out to siding contractors because we're often in front of the same homeowners. When someone finishes a washing job and asks about siding work, I'd rather send them to a contractor I trust.

Would a quick call make sense to see if there's a fit? We pay $50 for every job that closes from a referral, and we send jobs back the other way too."

### Cold Email to Realtor
Subject: "Homes listing in [County] — exterior cleaning"

"Hey [Name], I work with a handful of realtors across Northeast Ohio on pre-listing exterior clean-ups. House washing and driveway cleaning before photos go up can make a real difference in first impressions.

If you ever have a listing where the exterior needs attention, I'd like to be your go-to call. Fast turnaround, we serve all 7 Northeast Ohio counties.

Worth a quick chat?"

### LinkedIn Message (shorter)
"[Name] — I noticed you're doing siding work in [County]. We do exterior washing across the same area. A lot of my customers ask about siding and a lot of yours probably ask about cleaning.

Would it make sense to swap referrals? Happy to get on a 10-minute call."

## Tracking Partners in HubSpot
- Tag as "Referral Partner"
- Note: partner type (siding / landscaping / realtor / etc.), county, contact preference
- Log every referral sent and received
- Quarterly check-in reminder in HubSpot task
