# Before You List Your House:
## The Northeast Ohio Seller's Exterior Checklist

*What Cleveland-area real estate agents don't always tell you — and what buyers notice before they even walk through the door.*

### Why Curb Appeal Closes Deals

Studies consistently show that buyers form their impression of a home within the first 7 seconds of pulling up. Before they see the kitchen, the master bath, the finished basement — they see the roof, the siding, the driveway.

In Northeast Ohio, years of lake humidity, road salt, and weather cycles leave marks on every home exterior. Most sellers don't see it anymore because they've been looking at it every day.

Your buyer sees it fresh. And they notice.

This checklist is what we walk through at Forest City Power Washing before we quote a pre-listing job. Use it before your listing photos. Use it before your first showing. Use it before you drop the price.

---

## THE CHECKLIST

### ✅ Roof
- [ ] **Black or dark streaking on shingles?** That's gloeocapsa magma — a bacteria that feeds on limestone in asphalt shingles. Buyers who know what it is (many do) will immediately ask for a roof credit or inspection. Soft washing removes it completely.
- [ ] **Missing, curling, or lifted shingles?** Flag for your agent — buyers will notice and ask for a credit.
- [ ] **Moss or lichen growth?** More common on north-facing slopes. Soft wash removes it.
- [ ] **Staining around chimney flashing or skylights?** May indicate a leak; worth addressing before inspection.

**Forest City note:** Roof soft wash starts around $200–350 for a standard home. One buyer credit for "roof concerns" can run $2,000–5,000. The math is obvious.

---

### ✅ Siding
- [ ] **Green or gray tint on any exterior wall?** Algae — most visible on north and east-facing walls that get morning moisture.
- [ ] **Dark mold streaks below gutters or window frames?** Common and fully removable with soft wash.
- [ ] **Chalky white residue on brick or stone?** Efflorescence — mineral deposits. Cleanable with the right solution.
- [ ] **Staining around AC units or dryer vents?** Easy to miss; buyers and inspectors notice.

**Forest City note:** Full house soft wash removes algae, mold, and surface staining. One visit, one price. Most homes look dramatically different — listing photos are more impressive, and there's one less objection in the buyer's head.

---

### ✅ Driveway & Walkways
- [ ] **Salt staining, tire marks, or oil spots?** Ohio road salt leaves white/gray residue on concrete every winter. Pressure washing removes most of it.
- [ ] **Cracking or settling?** Note for disclosure — but get the surface clean first so the cracks are the only issue, not the grime on top of them.
- [ ] **Front walkway clean and inviting?** This is the path buyers walk to the front door. Make it look maintained.

**Forest City note:** Driveway + walkway cleaning starts around $149. This is one of the highest ROI items on this list — buyers and agents walk that path on every showing.

---

### ✅ Deck and Patio
- [ ] **Gray, weathered wood?** Means the deck is drying out. A wash and seal restores the color and shows buyers it's been maintained.
- [ ] **Mold or mildew on the surface?** Removable with soft wash.
- [ ] **Railings and stairs clean and stable?** Safety items get flagged on inspections.

**Forest City note:** Deck wash + seal is best done 2–3 weeks before listing (needs time to dry before photos).

---

### ✅ Gutters and Fascia
- [ ] **Gutters clean and draining?** Clogged gutters create visible overflow staining down the siding — a red flag for buyers wondering about maintenance.
- [ ] **Black streaking on the outside of gutters (tiger stripes)?** Oxidation from runoff. Removable with the right cleaner.
- [ ] **Fascia boards clean and painted?** Easy to miss from the ground, but visible in drone photos.

---

### ✅ Garage
- [ ] **Garage door clean?** One of the most visible surfaces from the street. A pressure wash and touch-up paint takes an hour.
- [ ] **Driveway apron (the section right at the garage)?** Gets the most traffic and the most staining.

---

### ✅ Overall Impression
- [ ] **Stand at the curb and take a photo on your phone.** This is what the listing photos will capture. Is the house the star, or is the grime?
- [ ] **Walk the perimeter once.** Look at what you've stopped noticing. Buyers will see it.
