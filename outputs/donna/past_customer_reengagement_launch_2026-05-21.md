## Step 1 — Build Your List (10 minutes)

Find past customers from:
1. **Workiz** — look at all "Done/Completed" jobs from 2024–2025
2. **Your phone** — anyone you've personally texted about a job
3. **Google Reviews** — anyone who left a review, you have their name

Target: 15–30 people. Prioritize customers who:
- Had a job done 12–24 months ago (due for another)
- Are homeowners (not renters)
- Left a positive review or said they'd call again

Write the list in a notes app with: Name | Phone | What job they had | Last service date

---

## Step 2 — Send the Text (Monday May 26, before noon)

Use the exact template from `outputs/tommy/past_customer_reengagement_2026-05-18.md`

**The text message (adapt to each person):**

> Hey [First Name], it's Bradley from Forest City Power Washing. We cleaned your [house/driveway/deck] last [year/spring] — wanted to check in and see if you're thinking about doing it again this season. We're booking now and I can usually get you on the schedule within 1–2 weeks. Worth a call?

**Rules:**
- Send from your personal cell, not a business line
- Personalize the first line — mention what you did for them
- Send max 5 at a time then wait 15 minutes (don't blast all at once — it looks like spam)
- Send between 9am–11am Monday for best reply rate

---

## Step 3 — Handle Replies (same day)

**YES / "Sure, what's the cost?"**
→ Give them a number on the spot or offer to come by for a quick look
→ If they commit verbally, text them the booking confirmation from `outputs/tommy/quote_to_close_kit_2026-05-20.md`

**"Maybe later"**
→ "No problem — I'll follow up in a few weeks. Slots tend to fill in June."
→ Set a reminder to text them June 9

**No reply by Day 3 (May 29)**
→ Send the email follow-up from the reengagement sequence
→ Subject: "Checking in — Forest City Power Washing"

**No reply after Day 7 (June 2)**
→ One final text from the sequence, then move on
→ They'll remember you next time
