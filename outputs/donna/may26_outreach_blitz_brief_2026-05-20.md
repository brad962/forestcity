## Why May 26 Is the Best Outreach Day of the Year

Memorial Day weekend, everyone's out. Property managers are at cookouts, not their desks. Contractors are with their families. Nothing happens Friday–Monday.

Then Tuesday May 26 hits.

Everyone is back. Their inboxes are emptier than normal (nobody sent anything over the long weekend). Their calendars are fresh. They're thinking about summer — what needs to get done before July. Decision cycles that were stalled over the long weekend restart.

**This is the single highest-response window of peak season.** 4-day pause in outreach = your message stands out instead of competing.
