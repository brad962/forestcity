## The Opportunity

Facebook ads launch Tuesday May 26. Some percentage of people who click the ad won't book immediately — they need more time, more proof, or they're just not ready. Without email capture, those people are **gone forever**. With email capture, they stay in your list and receive follow-up content until they're ready.

The **NE Ohio Home Maintenance Checklist** is the opt-in magnet. It's highly relevant to homeowners who just saw your ad, it's free, and it answers a question they actually have ("what should I be doing to maintain my house?").

## Step 1 — Create the Landing Page (10 minutes)

In Kit.com dashboard → **Landing Pages** → **New Landing Page** → choose the simplest template

**Headline:**
> Your Free NE Ohio Home Maintenance Checklist

**Subhead:**
> The 12-month exterior care guide for Cleveland-area homeowners. What to check, what to fix, and when — so your house looks good and stays protected all year.

**Bullet points (3 is enough):**
- Month-by-month checklist built for Northeast Ohio's climate
- When to wash, when to seal, when to call a pro
- Includes the roof warning signs most homeowners miss until it's too late

**Email form label:** "Where should we send it?"

**Button text:** "Send me the checklist"

**No name field** — just email. Fewer fields = more conversions.

**Thank-you message (after they submit):**
> Your checklist is on its way. Check your inbox in the next 5 minutes. While you wait — if your house is already showing any of the signs in the checklist (green siding, black roof streaks, or a driveway that hasn't been cleaned in years), we can usually fit you in within 2 weeks. [link to your website or contact page]

## Step 2 — Set Up Automated Delivery (5 minutes)

In Kit.com → **Automations** → **New Automation**

Trigger: "Subscriber joins a form" → select your landing page form

Action: **"Send email"**

**Subject line:**
> Your NE Ohio Home Maintenance Checklist (+ a tip for May)

**Email body:**
> Hi [FirstName],
>
> Here's your checklist: [paste checklist content OR attach as PDF OR link to a Google Doc version]
>
> Since it's May, the most important section right now is **March–May** — the algae from the winter is visible by now, and the peak cleaning window (before summer heat bakes it in) is the next 3–4 weeks.
>
> If your siding looks green or your roof has dark streaks, that's exactly what we handle. We're currently booking into June. Free quote — just call or text [your number].
>
> — Bradley
> Forest City Power Washing
> [your phone] | [website]

## Step 3 — Paste the Checklist Content (5 minutes)

Two options:

**Option A (fastest):** Copy the content from `outputs/tommy/home_maintenance_checklist_content_2026-05-24.md` and paste it into a Google Doc. Share as "Anyone with the link can view." Paste the Google Doc link into the delivery email above.

**Option B (higher perceived value):** Copy the content into a simple Canva document and export as PDF. Upload the PDF to Kit.com's file hosting and share the download link in the delivery email.

Option A is faster. Option B looks more professional. Do Option A first to get it live, switch to Option B later.

## Step 4 — Set Up the 3-Touch Nurture Sequence (10 minutes)

The nurture sequence already exists: `outputs/donna/nurture_sequence_home_maintenance_checklist_2026-05-12.md`

In Kit.com → **Automations** → add these emails after the delivery email:

**Email 2 (Day 3 after opt-in):**
Subject: "This is the most common thing people miss on their NE Ohio homes"
Content: Short educational email about roof soft washing. Why pressure washing is wrong. What the black streaks actually are. CTA: free quote link.

**Email 3 (Day 7 after opt-in):**
Subject: "Before and after: what a $299 house wash looks like"
Content: Use a real before/after photo. Show what they're living with vs. what it could look like. CTA: book a quote.
