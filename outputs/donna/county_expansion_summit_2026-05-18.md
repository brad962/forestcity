## Why Summit County Now

- **Week 21 rotation** — Danny's Apollo pull is live this week in Summit County (Akron, Fairlawn, Stow)
- **Market size**: Akron is the 5th largest city in Ohio. More property managers and HOA communities than any county in our rotation except Cuyahoga
- **Competition**: Regional power washing companies serve Akron, but most are Cuyahoga-focused — Summit County feels like an afterthought to them. That's an opening.
- **Distance from Cleveland**: ~40 min. Operationally feasible. Same-day scheduling is realistic.
- **Lake moisture effect**: Summit County sits south of the lake effect zone — slightly less algae than Cuyahoga, but road salt staining is significant. Driveway + concrete jobs are strong.
