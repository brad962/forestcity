## Channel 1: Facebook Homeowner Groups (Highest Priority — 20 min)

### The 5 Groups to Post In Today

Search these in Facebook. Join if not already a member, then post:

1. **Cleveland Home Improvement** — large, active, homeowners asking for vendor recs constantly
2. **Northeast Ohio Homeowners** — broad NE Ohio group, seasonal services get real traction in May
3. **Cuyahoga County Community Board** — local neighborhood-feel, small business posts do well
4. **Strongsville / Parma / Lakewood community pages** — hyper-local = higher trust
5. **NE Ohio Real Estate Investors** — landlords with multiple properties, commercial potential

### The Post (Copy-Paste Ready)

> Anyone else's siding looking rough after this winter?
>
> We've been washing houses, driveways, and roofs across Cuyahoga, Lake, and Lorain Counties all spring. The algae buildup this year is bad — the moisture from the lake doesn't help.
>
> Before-and-after from last week 👇 [attach before/after photo from outputs/jasmine/]
>
> If you're in Northeast Ohio and want a free quote, drop your zip code in the comments or DM me. Booking into June now.
>
> — Bradley, Forest City Power Washing

**Post rules:**
- Include a before/after photo (use any file from `outputs/jasmine/`)
- Post it as YOUR personal profile, not a business page — personal posts get 5–10x the organic reach
- Reply to every comment within 30 minutes while the post is fresh

### When Someone Comments/DMs — Copy-Paste Reply

**For "what do you charge?" or "how much?"**
> Hey [name]! Pricing depends on the size of your home and what you need done. A standard house wash runs $150–$350 for most NE Ohio homes. Happy to give you an exact quote — what's your address and zip code?

**For "are you insured?"**
> Yep — fully insured and licensed in Ohio. We can send proof of insurance with your estimate if you need it.

**For "can you do [specific surface]?"**
> Yes — we do house washing, driveways, decks, roofs (soft wash), fences, and commercial properties. What are you looking to get done?

## Channel 2: Nextdoor (15 min to set up — then 5 min/post)

### One-Time Setup

1. Go to **nextdoor.com/business**
2. Create a free "Neighborhood Favorite" business page
3. Business name: Forest City Power Washing
4. Add: phone number, website (if live), service area (Northeast Ohio)
5. Upload logo + 1–2 before/after photos as your cover images

### The Post (after setup)

Post this in your local neighborhood AND the 2–3 surrounding neighborhoods:

> Hi neighbors — I run Forest City Power Washing and we're booking spring cleans across NE Ohio right now.
>
> Great timing to get your driveway, siding, or deck cleaned before summer cookout season. Before-and-after results are dramatic — especially on driveways with winter road salt staining.
>
> We serve Cuyahoga, Lake, and Lorain Counties. Mention Nextdoor and get 10% off your first service.
>
> Free quotes: [your phone number]

**Why the discount works:** Nextdoor users are hyperlocal and deal-motivated. The "mention Nextdoor" code lets you track which leads came from this channel.

### Post cadence: 1x/week through June

## Channel 4: Ask Past Customers for Referrals (Not Reviews — Referrals)

Different from review requests. This is a personal text to past customers asking if they know anyone who needs power washing.

**Text to send (to anyone you've done work for in the last 12 months):**

> Hey [name] — hope you're doing well! Spring is here and we're booked up with cleanups across [their area]. Quick ask — do you know anyone who's been wanting their house or driveway washed? I'll knock $50 off their job if they mention your name. And another $25 for you as a thank-you.
>
> No pressure — just thought of you! — Bradley

**Target list:** Pull from Workiz jobs (any status = "Paid" or "Done"). Aim for 10–15 texts. Revenue math: 3 referrals closed = $600–$900. Takes 15 minutes.
