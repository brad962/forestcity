## HOW TO USE NEXTDOOR (10 minutes to set up)
1. Go to nextdoor.com — create an account as a local business (or as Bradley personally in your neighborhood)
2. Claim the business listing under "Forest City Power Washing"
3. Post in the "Recommendations" or "Local Business" section
4. Post in your neighborhood AND neighboring ones (Nextdoor lets you reach adjacent neighborhoods)

## NEXTDOOR STRATEGY (5 posts/month = free lead flow)
- Post once a week in 3–5 neighborhoods simultaneously
- Respond to EVERY recommendation request for pressure washing (search "pressure washing" in Nextdoor)
- When someone asks "does anyone know a good pressure washer?" — answer within 1 hour
- Post before/after photos (Nextdoor loves photos — they get more reach than text)
- Build up reviews: after each job, text customer: "If you're happy with the work, it'd mean a lot if you posted a quick recommendation on Nextdoor — takes 2 minutes."
