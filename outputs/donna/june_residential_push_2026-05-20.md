- Before/after Facebook photos are at their highest engagement all year

Assets are already built. The only missing piece was this coordinated plan.

## The One Decision That Unlocks Everything

> **Build the service pages first. Every other channel converges on them.**

The Facebook ad says "get a free quote." It clicks through to a landing page. If that page is a generic homepage — you lose the conversion. If it's a "House Washing — Northeast Ohio" page with a quote form, proof, and pricing signals — you close it.

15 years of power washing advertising data says: 70% of conversion improvement comes from the landing page, 30% from the ad.

**Priority:** Roof Soft Wash page first (highest-value, highest search intent, most fear-based purchase). House Washing second.
