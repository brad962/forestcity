## Mid-June Seasonal Language Map

### What customers are saying THIS WEEK (June 15–21):

- "Father's Day is Sunday and the house looks terrible"
- "I need it done before the Fourth"
- "We have family coming in from out of town in two weeks"
- "My realtor said to do it before we list in September — why wait?"
- "It's been the same color since winter and now I can't stop seeing it"
- "The algae is climbing up the north side — it's way up now"

### The July 4th Countdown Window:
July 4th is 19 days out. This is the second-biggest residential deadline of the season. Customers booking NOW for the Fourth are motivated buyers — they have a hard deadline, they're not price-shopping. CTA language to run June 15–28:
- "4th of July slots are going fast"
- "Beat the Fourth — book this week"
- "Want it done before the holiday weekend? We have [X] slots left"

### What NOT to say in mid-June:
- "Father's Day special" after June 21 — drops dead Monday the 22nd
- "Before summer" — it IS summer, sounds out of touch
- Anything implying they have lots of time to decide

### Replace stale phrases with:
| Retire | Use Instead |
|--------|------------|
| "Get ready for summer" | "Don't spend the whole summer looking at it" |
| "Spring into clean" | "Beat the Fourth of July — book this week" |
| "Schedule your spring wash" | "July 4th weekend slots are filling" |
| "Clean before the season starts" | "It's mid-June — here's how fast we can fix it" |
