# Cleveland Area Competitor Analysis
### Power Washing Market — Northeast Ohio
*Marcus Webb | Market Intelligence Analyst | 2026-05-12*

---

## Summary

Researched the top active competitors in the Greater Cleveland / Northeast Ohio power washing market. Five companies profiled below. Key findings:

1. **All-Star Power Cleaning** is the closest direct competitor — same geography, same soft wash emphasis, similar service mix. Strong review presence.
2. **Clearview SoftWash** is dominant in Lake County — direct competition in Mentor/Willoughby area. Founded 2001, highest review count, family-owned.
3. **Pristine Clean** covers Cleveland AND Columbus — larger operation, more corporate feel, PWNA certified, runs discount promos.
4. **Brothers Powerwash** — NE Ohio regional, professional, soft wash capable.
5. **Devins Powerwash** — Cleveland-based, lower profile, local competition.

**Forest City's opening:** Most competitors don't strongly emphasize their local, personal identity the way Forest City can. "Bradley shows up" is a differentiator — a named person, not a faceless crew.
