## What Customers Are Thinking Right Now (May–June)

These are the real mental triggers active in a Northeast Ohio homeowner's head during peak season. Use this language in ads, posts, and email sequences.

---

### Trigger 1 — "I Can Finally See It"

Spring sunlight hits the house at a different angle. After a gray Ohio winter, homeowners step outside, squint at the siding, and see the green for the first time.

**Their words:** *"I didn't notice it all winter and then one morning I looked up and thought — oh no."*

**Use in:** Facebook posts, ad hooks, email subject lines
**Hook:** "Spring is when you finally see what winter did to your siding."

---

### Trigger 2 — "We're Having People Over"

Memorial Day. Mother's Day. Graduation parties. The summer calendar is filling up. Homeowners suddenly care intensely about how the house looks from the street.

**Their words:** *"We have people coming over for Memorial Day and I can't have the house looking like this."*

**Use in:** Facebook urgency posts, Google ad headlines, Tommy's estimate follow-up
**Hook:** "Company's coming. Is your house ready?"

---

### Trigger 3 — "The Neighbors Just Got Theirs Done"

Social comparison is the single biggest trigger for booking in residential power washing. One clean house on the block makes the dirty ones obvious.

**Their words:** *"Saw the Hendersons got their house done. I've been meaning to call someone."*

**Use in:** Jasmine's Facebook posts (reference neighborhood), Nextdoor posts, Rick's lead gen ads
**Hook:** "One house on every block gets done first. Make it yours."

---

### Trigger 4 — "I've Been Putting This Off"

Guilt is a real driver. Many homeowners know they need it done. They've been "meaning to call." Procrastination is something you can call out directly in copy.

**Their words:** *"I've been meaning to do this for two years."*

**Use in:** Rick's retargeting ads, Tommy's urgency CTA, Jasmine's "before summer" post
**Hook:** "You've been meaning to do this. Here's how to stop waiting."

---

### Trigger 5 — "I Don't Want to Damage My Roof"

Fear-based. Homeowners who've been burned by bad contractors, or who've read about pressure washing damage, are cautious. Soft wash education is a trust builder.

**Their words:** *"I was worried about someone messing up the shingles."*

**Use in:** Tommy's services pages, Jasmine's educational post, Danny's PM sequence (property liability angle)
**Hook:** "We don't use pressure on roofs. Here's why."

---

### Trigger 6 — "I Want to Sell / I'm Listing Soon"

Realtor referral trigger. Sellers need curb appeal immediately. Listing timeline = urgency.

**Their words:** *"We're listing in 3 weeks. What can you do?"*

**Use in:** Carla's realtor outreach, Tommy's "Before You List" content, Donna's lead magnet
**Hook:** "Listing soon? We can turn it around in 48 hours."

## What Property Managers Are Thinking Right Now

Different audience, different triggers:

- **Annual budget cycles**: May/June is when Q2 maintenance gets executed
- **Tenant renewal season**: Visible property condition affects lease renewals
- **Board meeting prep**: HOA communities need to show the board they're maintaining the asset
- **Portfolio efficiency**: "Can you handle all 4 of my properties in one day?"

**Words that work with PMs:** *efficiency, documentation, same-day photo report, one call covers everything, volume pricing*
