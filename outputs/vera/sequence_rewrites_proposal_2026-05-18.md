## TOUCH 1 REWRITE — Property Manager Sequence

**Current (assumed):** Introduces Forest City, explains what we do, generic CTA.

**Proposed rewrite:**

> Subject: Quick question for [Company]
>
> Hi [firstName],
>
> We do exterior cleaning — power washing, soft wash, roof cleaning — for property managers across NE Ohio.
>
> Quick question: does [Company] have a vendor you call for that, or is it usually whoever picks up the phone first?
>
> Just trying to understand if there's a gap we could fill for you.
>
> Bradley Neal
> Forest City Power Washing
> (XXX) XXX-XXXX
>
> P.S. If exterior cleaning isn't on your radar right now, just say the word and I'll get out of your inbox.

**Why it works:** Ends with a yes/no question — reply threshold is as low as possible. P.S. gives them an easy exit which paradoxically gets more replies.
