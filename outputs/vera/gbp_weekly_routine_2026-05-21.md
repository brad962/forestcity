## The 30-Minute Weekly Routine (every Monday)

### Step 1 — Post one photo (5 min)
- Go to: business.google.com → Photos → Add photo
- Upload a before/after (from `outputs/jasmine/`) or a job photo from your phone
- Caption: "[City], [County] — [service type]. Forest City Power Washing, serving 7 counties."

### Step 2 — Post one offer/update (10 min)
Use one of the caption templates from `outputs/vera/gbp_post_may21_2026.md`

Best performing GBP post formats for home services:
1. **Before/after job photo** — highest engagement, shows proof of work
2. **Seasonal offer** — "Booking into June now — get on the schedule"
3. **FAQ post** — "Why soft wash instead of pressure wash for roofs?"
4. **5-star review screenshot** — social proof directly in Maps results

### Step 3 — Reply to any new reviews (5 min)
- Reply to every review within 24h — Google rewards this with higher ranking
- Template for positive review reply: "Thanks [Name]! Glad we could make the [house/driveway/deck] look great. Call us anytime — we serve the whole [county] area."
- Template for any negative: "Hi [Name], I'm sorry to hear this. Can you DM me or call [phone]? I want to make it right."

### Step 4 — Check Q&A section (5 min)
- Look for any new questions on your profile
- Answer promptly — unanswered Q&As hurt ranking

### Step 5 — Update booking link (5 min, one-time setup)
- Go to GBP → Info → Appointment links
- Add your booking URL or Workiz scheduling link
- Once set, every Maps search result shows a "Book" button — direct calls and clicks

## Quick SEO Notes

- In every GBP post, mention at least one city/county name (helps for local search)
- Mention the service type explicitly: "soft wash," "power washing," "house washing," "driveway cleaning"
- Link to your website in the post body (if GBP allows links in posts)
- GBP posts expire after 7 days — the weekly routine keeps the profile "active" in Google's eyes
