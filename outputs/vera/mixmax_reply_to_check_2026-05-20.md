## Why This Matters

If 45 contacts opened your emails but zero replied, one possible explanation is the Reply-To field is set to an address you don't check (or an address that bounces). A single 3-minute check confirms whether replies are even reaching you.

---

## Step-by-Step Check

**1. Open Mixmax**
Go to: https://app.mixmax.com → Click **Sequences** in the left sidebar

**2. Open the Property Manager Sequence**
Sequence name: "Forest City Power Washing – NE Ohio Property Managers"

**3. Go to Settings**
Click the **Settings** tab (or gear icon) for the sequence → Look for **Sender Settings** or **Reply-To**

**4. Verify the Reply-To address**
It should match: your actual inbox (bradley@forestcitypowerwashing.com or your Gmail)

If it shows:
- ✅ **Your address** — replies are routing correctly
- ⚠️ **A different address** — fix it before Touch 3 fires (May 22)
- ⚠️ **Blank / empty** — Mixmax defaults to the sending email, which is usually fine, but confirm it's your real inbox

**5. Check the sending email**
Click your profile → Settings → **Connected Email Accounts**
Confirm the address shows as verified and connected.

---

## If Replies Are Routing Wrong

1. Click the sequence → Settings → Reply-To field → update to your real inbox
2. Click Save
3. Done. All future replies (including Touch 3) will route to the correct inbox.

---

## Also Check: Spam Folder

Gmail sometimes routes "commercial-looking" emails to Promotions/Spam.
- Search your Gmail for: `from:mixmax` or `in:all "replied to your sequence"`
- If replies went to spam: right-click one → "Not spam" → filter sender to go to inbox
