## The Psychology of "Closing the Loop"

Most people who don't respond aren't hostile — they're just busy and procrastinating. A "last follow-up" message removes the pressure and often triggers the reply that nothing else did. The key elements:
1. No ask — just closing the loop
2. No guilt — genuinely friendly
3. Leave the door open for later
4. Very short — 2 sentences max
