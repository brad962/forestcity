## The Concept

After every power washing job, leave door hangers on **3–5 neighboring houses** within 100 feet.

The freshly washed house is visible proof. Neighbors can literally see what you just did. This is the highest-converting physical marketing in the service industry — 3–5% door hanger conversion rate vs. 0.5% for cold mailers.

**This weekend cost: $0** — you likely already have what you need (or $15 at FedEx Office).

## Deployment Protocol

### When to Drop Hangers
- **During or immediately after every job** — the house looks clean, the contrast is visible
- **Target houses:** Direct neighbors (both sides + 2 across the street)
- **Skip:** Houses with "No Soliciting" signs — respect it

### What to Say If Someone Comes Out
> "Hey, I just finished washing your neighbor's house — it's the one right there. Wanted to leave you something in case you ever needed the same thing. We're local, we're fast."

This is not a pitch. It's a conversation starter. People WILL come out — they've been watching you work.

### Memorial Day Weekend Specific
This weekend, neighborhoods are FULL of people doing outdoor projects and hosting cookouts. They are looking at their house with fresh eyes. Door hanger timing is perfect.

- **Saturday May 24:** After any job you do — drop 5 hangers
- **Saturday drive-arounds:** If you have no job booked, drive through 2 NE Ohio neighborhoods that look like they need washing. Drop 10 hangers each. Note the addresses in your phone.
- **Sunday May 25:** Same — drive through 1–2 neighborhoods after church or in the afternoon

## Scale-Up Version (June Blitz)

Once you've tested this weekend:
1. Print 200–500 hangers (cost: $50–$100 at FedEx or VistaPrint)
2. After EVERY job, drop 10 hangers in the neighborhood
3. On slow days, do a dedicated 90-minute neighborhood drop in a target area
4. Pair with the Google Reviews ask — clean house + door hanger = their neighbors see BOTH the clean house AND the review

**Revenue math:**
- 200 hangers → 6–10 callbacks (3–5% rate)
- 6–10 callbacks → 3–5 booked jobs (50% close rate)
- 3–5 jobs × $400 avg = $1,200–$2,000 per 200 hangers
- Cost: $50 for hangers + 2 hours of drop time
