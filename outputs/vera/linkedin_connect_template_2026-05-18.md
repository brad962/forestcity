# LinkedIn Connect Message — Hot Leads Protocol
*Vera Cole | 2026-05-18 | Ready to use — no edits needed*

---

## When to Send
Send a LinkedIn connect request to any contact who has opened your email **2+ times** but not replied.
Nina's daily hot leads report now includes a "Connect" link for each hot lead — click it and send this message.

**Timing:** Within 48 hours of seeing them on the hot leads report.

---

## The Message (under 300 characters — works in LinkedIn's 300-char connect limit)

> Hey [First Name] — I've been reaching out about Forest City Power Washing. We do commercial exterior work across NE Ohio. Figured I'd connect here too in case the timing was better. No pressure at all.

**If they accept without replying, follow up 3 days later:**

> Thanks for connecting! Just wanted to say — if exterior cleaning ever comes up for [Company], we work with property managers and facility managers across the area. Happy to give you a quick quote any time.

---

## For Realtors (if the hot lead is a realtor)

> Hey [First Name] — sent you a note about Forest City Power Washing. We help listings look their best before photos and showings. Wanted to connect here too. Happy to chat if you have a listing coming up.

---

## For Contractors (if the hot lead is a contractor)

> Hey [First Name] — I reached out about a referral partnership between Forest City Power Washing and [Company]. We refer each other and split the wins. Wanted to connect here if you're open to a quick conversation.

---

## Rules
- Never lead with a hard pitch in the connect request — this kills acceptance rates
- Keep it under 300 characters for the initial note
- If they accept but don't respond, wait 3 days before the follow-up message
- Log the outreach in pipeline_data.json (stage: "LinkedIn Contacted")
