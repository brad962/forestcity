## Setup — Desktop Notifications (Chrome)

### Step 1 — Open Mixmax Settings
1. Open Gmail in Chrome
2. Click the Mixmax icon (top right, looks like a lightning bolt or "M")
3. Click **Settings** → **Notifications**

### Step 2 — Enable Open Notifications
Under **Email Tracking Notifications**, check:
- ✅ **Notify me when an email is opened**
- ✅ **Notify me in browser** (Chrome popup)
- ✅ **Notify me by email** (optional — only if you want an email log too)

Under **Sequence Notifications**, check:
- ✅ **Notify when a sequence recipient opens an email**

### Step 3 — Allow Chrome Notifications
If Chrome asks for permission: click **Allow**.

If you never got the Chrome prompt:
- Chrome address bar → click the lock icon → **Notifications** → **Allow**

### Step 4 — Test It
- Open your Sent folder
- Click any Mixmax-tracked email
- The "eye" icon next to it shows opens
- Chrome will show a popup notification in the future

## Setup — Mobile (iPhone/Android)

### iPhone — Gmail + Mixmax
Mixmax doesn't have a standalone app. The notifications come through Chrome on desktop.

**For mobile alerts**, use the Mixmax Gmail integration:
1. Mixmax Settings → Notifications → **Send me an email when a recipient opens**
2. Gmail mobile → **Turn on Gmail notifications** (Settings → Notifications → All)
3. Now when someone opens your email, you get a Gmail notification on your phone

### Even Simpler — Set Up a Gmail Filter
1. Gmail → Settings → Filters and Blocked Addresses → **Create new filter**
2. From: `notifications@mixmax.com`
3. Subject contains: `opened`
4. Mark as important + Apply label: `🔥 MIXMAX OPEN`
5. On mobile, set Gmail to notify for "Important" mail

Result: your phone buzzes when Mixmax detects a sequence open.

## Troubleshooting

**"I'm not seeing open notifications"**
→ Make sure Chrome is running in foreground, or use the Gmail email notification method above.

**"The Mixmax icon isn't showing in Gmail"**
→ Chrome Extensions → Check that Mixmax is enabled → Reload Gmail

**"I use Firefox / Safari"**
→ Mixmax only works in Chrome. Use Chrome for Gmail today.
