# Instagram Stories Scripts — Peak Season 2026
### Created: 2026-05-24 | Jasmine Burke — Social Media Manager
*5 Stories scripts for May–June. Each is 15–30 seconds, shot on phone, no editing required.*
*Post to: Instagram → Your Story → Camera or Gallery upload*

## Why Instagram Stories Now
Instagram Stories are seen by more followers than feed posts. Before/after content in Stories creates a "reveal" moment that gets DMs. 500M people watch Stories daily. In peak season (May–June), "outdoor home projects" content gets 3–5x normal engagement.

**Frequency target: 3–5 Stories per week.** Each job is content. Each before shot is content.

---

## Story 1 — THE BEFORE REVEAL
*Works best with any job you're arriving at. Shoot before you start.*

**Slide 1** (photo of dirty siding/roof/driveway):
> Text overlay: "This is what we showed up to today 😬"
> Sticker: 📍 [City, Ohio]

**Slide 2** (photo of equipment being set up or crew working):
> Text overlay: "One hour later…"

**Slide 3** (after photo — clean surface):
> Text overlay: "Same house. Different story. 💪"

**Slide 4** (selfie or text slide with logo):
> Text overlay: "We're booking June now — DM us for a quote"
> Add a **Link sticker** → your website or phone number

*30-second total. Shot entirely on iPhone. No editing needed.*

## Story 2 — THE ROOF REVEAL
*High-impact because most people don't know soft washing exists.*

**Slide 1** (before — black streaks on roof):
> Text overlay: "That's not just dirt 👀"
> Sticker: QUESTION — "Do you know what causes black roof streaks?"

*Wait 24 hrs, show the results slide:*

**Slide 2** (after — clean roof):
> Text overlay: "It's algae. And it's destroying your shingles."
> "We removed it — no pressure, no damage, just soft wash chemistry."

**Slide 3**:
> Text overlay: "Northeast Ohio roofs deal with this every spring. Yours might too."
> "DM me 'ROOF' for a quote" → Add DM link sticker

*The question sticker drives engagement and bumps the Story in the algorithm.*

## Story 3 — WHILE WE'RE HERE (Neighbor Hook)
*Use after finishing any job. Shoot from the street.*

**Slide 1** (photo of finished house from across the street):
> Text overlay: "[Neighborhood/City] — job complete ✅"

**Slide 2** (text slide — white background):
> Large text: "If you live near [cross streets or neighborhood] in [city]..."
> Smaller: "We're in the area this week. Same-day or next-day discount if we can add you to the route."

**Slide 3**:
> Text overlay: "DM us the word NEIGHBOR and we'll tell you availability."
> Add: LINK sticker → your phone or website

*This Story + the neighbor canvass script = free residential add-ons every single job.*

---

## Story 4 — CUSTOMER REACTION (Social Proof)
*Capture this at the end of every job. Takes 45 seconds.*

**Slide 1** (after photo):
> Text overlay: "[First name] in [City] — here's what they said:"

**Slide 2** (screenshot or typed quote from customer text/review):
> If they texted you something like "Wow it looks amazing" → screenshot it
> Or type it as a quote over a clean background

**Slide 3**:
> Text overlay: "We treat every job like it's on a busy street. Because it usually is."
> "Booking June → DM QUOTE"

*Ask every customer after the job: "Would you mind texting me what you thought?" — that gives you Story 4 content every time.*

## Story 5 — SEASONAL URGENCY
*Post any Friday May–June to drive weekend bookings.*

**Slide 1** (clean home exterior photo):
> Text overlay: "Beautiful weather this weekend ☀️"
> "Your neighbors are going to be outside."

**Slide 2** (text slide):
> "We have [X] spots left this week."
> "House wash, driveway, deck — or all three."

**Slide 3**:
> Text overlay: "Book by tonight, we schedule this weekend."
> Add LINK sticker + phone number

*Change "X spots" to actual availability. Creates real scarcity.*

## Technical Notes
- **Best resolution:** 1080 x 1920 (iPhone portrait mode — just shoot vertical)
- **Text placement:** Keep text in the middle third — top and bottom get cut off on some devices
- **Hashtags in Stories:** Add 3–5 hashtags in small white text at bottom or use Location sticker
  - #ClevelandOhio #PowerWashing #NortheastOhio #CurbAppeal #BeforeAndAfter
- **Frequency:** Stories disappear in 24 hours — more is better. Aim for 3x/week minimum
- **Highlights:** Save the best before/after Stories to a Highlight called "Results" so new profile visitors see them
