## Step 1 — Create the Account (5 min)

1. On your phone → App Store → download **Instagram** (if not installed)
2. Open Instagram → **Sign up**
3. Email: use the business email (not personal)
4. Username: `forestcitypowerwashing` (first choice) or `forestcitypw` if taken
5. Password: use your standard business password

---

## Step 2 — Switch to Business Account (2 min)

1. Tap your profile icon (bottom right)
2. Tap the 3 lines (top right) → **Settings and privacy**
3. Tap **Account type and tools**
4. Tap **Switch to professional account**
5. Select **Business** → category: **Cleaning Service**
6. Link to your Facebook Page when prompted

---
