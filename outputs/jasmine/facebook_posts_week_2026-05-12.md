## Post 2 — Educational (Wednesday)

**Caption:**

Can I pressure wash my own roof?

Short answer: please don't.

Here's what happens when you point a pressure washer at asphalt shingles:
→ The granules get stripped off (those protect against UV and impact)
→ Water gets forced under the shingles and into the wood decking
→ Your roof warranty gets voided — most manufacturers are explicit about this

Soft washing is the right method. Low pressure, professional-grade cleaning solution, rinses clean. The algae dies. The roof stays intact.

This is the only way we clean roofs. If a company is pressure washing roofs, walk away.
