### Post 2 — Wednesday June 11 (Educational — Roof Soft Wash)

If your roof looks black from the street, that's not age. It's alive.

That dark streaking is Gloeocapsa magma — a bacteria that feeds on the limestone in asphalt shingles. It's slowly shortening your roof's life. Left alone, it can take 5–10 years off a roof that should last 25–30.

Pressure washing won't fix it. It strips shingle granules and the bacteria grows back in 6 months.

Soft wash roof cleaning kills the bacteria at the source. Low pressure, the right solution. The streaks wash off. It doesn't come back for 3–5 years in a typical NE Ohio climate.

NE Ohio homeowners: if your roof has been looking dark, this is what's going on.
