## Caption Formula (every post)

1. **Hook** — stop-scroll first line (question, surprising stat, local reference)
2. **Body** — 2-3 short paragraphs, no fluff
3. **Local reference** — county or city name
4. **CTA** — soft: "DM us," "comment QUOTE," "link in bio"
5. **Hashtags** — 3-5 max: #ClevelandOhio #NortheastOhio #PowerWashing + 1-2 relevant
