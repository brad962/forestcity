## Post 2 — Educational
**Best day to post: Tuesday July 1**

Your deck took a beating this winter. Here's what to do before you ruin it trying to fix it.

Northeast Ohio road salt, freeze-thaw cycles, and six months of moisture do a number on wood surfaces. A lot of homeowners grab a pressure washer and go at it — and we get it, it's satisfying. But high pressure on wood grain raises the fibers, strips the surface, and can split older boards. Then you're staining a deck that looks worse than when you started. Soft washing a deck means low pressure and the right cleaning solution — it pulls the grime, mold, and mildew out of the wood without wrecking it. After that it's ready to stain or seal properly.

We soft wash decks and fences across all 7 counties. One visit, done right.
