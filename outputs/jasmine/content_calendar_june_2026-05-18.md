### Wednesday June 18 — Facebook
**Format:** Educational — Soft Wash vs Pressure Wash
**Hook:** "The difference that most homeowners don't know about"
**Post:**
> Most homeowners hear "power washing" and think one thing.
>
> But there's a big difference between power/pressure washing and soft washing.
>
> Pressure washing: high pressure, great for concrete and hard surfaces. Not safe for roofs or some siding types.
>
> Soft washing: low pressure + biodegradable cleaning solution. Kills algae, mold, and mildew at the source. Lasts longer because you're treating the problem, not just blasting the surface.
>
> We use both methods. We'll tell you which is right for your home.
>
