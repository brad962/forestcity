## Why Launch Now (Not Later)
- Peak season jobs happening now = fresh before/after content every week
- Memorial Day weekend → July 4 is the highest-engagement window for home services on Instagram
- Before/after content on Instagram gets 3-5x more saves than Facebook equivalents
- Competitors in NE Ohio power washing are NOT on Instagram — first-mover advantage now

## Step 4: Posting Rhythm (Ongoing)

**Frequency:** 3-4 posts per week during peak season (May–August)
**Best times:** Tuesday–Thursday, 11am–1pm or 7–9pm
**Minimum:** 1 post per week to keep the algorithm alive

**Content rotation:**
- Week 1: Before/after → educational → social proof
- Week 2: Before/after → behind-the-scenes → before/after
- Week 3: Before/after → commercial angle → seasonal hook
- Repeat

Use `instagram_stories_scripts_2026-05-24.md` for Stories every week (1-3 per week).

## Step 5: DM Response Protocol

When someone DMs "how much?" or "quote" or "interested":

**Response template:**
```
Hey [name]! Thanks for reaching out.

What service are you looking for — house wash, roof, driveway, or the full exterior?

And what city/county are you in? We cover all 7 NE Ohio counties.
```

Once they respond, collect the job details and text them a number. Don't try to close on Instagram DM — move them to text or phone within 2 messages.

## Quick Wins — First 30 Days

1. **Follow 10 NE Ohio home improvement accounts** — they'll follow back, exposing you to their audience
2. **Tag the city in every post** — "@clevelandohio" in the first comment gets local exposure
3. **Use Instagram's "Add Yours" sticker** in Stories — "Before/after of your home" chain gets shares
4. **Post a Story the day of every job** — 15-second time-lapse or before/during/after sequence
5. **Cross-post every Instagram post to Facebook** — takes 2 seconds, doubles reach

---

## What Not to Do

- Don't post more than once per day in the first month (hurts algorithm learning)
- Don't use generic hashtags like #home or #cleaning — too broad to get found
- Don't delete posts if they underperform — early algorithm needs content history
- Don't automate DM responses — Instagram penalizes bots; reply manually
- Don't launch without at least 3 posts already published — empty grids get zero follows
