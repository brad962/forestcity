## Post 2 — Educational
**Hook:** Your roof has years of algae on it — and pressure washing it would be the worst thing you could do.

We hear this all the time: "Can't I just rent a pressure washer?" For your driveway, sure. But for your roof, a pressure washer strips the granules off your shingles, can void your manufacturer warranty, and doesn't actually kill the algae — it just knocks it around.

Soft washing is the only safe method for roofs. Low-pressure water plus a biodegradable cleaning solution that actually kills what's growing up there. The algae stays gone longer, your shingles stay intact, and you're not gambling with your warranty.
