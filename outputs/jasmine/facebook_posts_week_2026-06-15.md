## Post 2 — Educational
**Best day to post: Tuesday June 17**

Your roof has algae on it right now. In June. And pressure washing it would make it worse.

Here's the thing most homeowners don't know: those black streaks on your shingles are a bacteria called Gloeocapsa magma. High-pressure water knocks it around but doesn't kill it — and it strips the granules off your shingles in the process. Voiding your warranty is the bonus insult. Soft washing uses low pressure + a biodegradable cleaning solution that actually kills the algae at the root. That's the only way to make it stay gone.

We soft wash every roof we touch. No exceptions.
