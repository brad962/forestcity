## Why This Segment

Real estate investors and house flippers are Carla's **highest-value untapped segment**. Here's why:

| Metric | Regular Realtor | REI Investor |
|--------|----------------|--------------|
| Jobs per relationship | 1–2/year | 4–12/year |
| Speed needed | "Can you do next week?" | "Can you do Monday?" |
| Price sensitivity | Medium | Low (it's a business cost) |
| Repeat business | Low | High — they flip all year |
| Referral value | Single home at a time | Multiple flips, other investors |

**NE Ohio REI market:** Cleveland and surrounding counties have one of the highest fix-and-flip concentrations in the Midwest. Low purchase prices + strong rental demand = active investor community.

---

## Target Profile

**Who:** Real estate investors, house flippers, BRRRR strategy investors, buy-and-hold landlords who manage their own properties

**Why they need us:**
- Every distressed home they buy needs exterior wash before listing (curb appeal = sale price)
- Tenants move out → property needs full exterior reset before re-renting
- Investor holds a portfolio of 5–20 rentals → needs seasonal maintenance on all of them
- Speed matters: they're on a timeline (carrying costs add up fast)

## Where to Find REI Investors (Beyond Apollo)

1. **BiggerPockets.com** — forums sorted by market. Cleveland/NE Ohio has active threads. Introduce Forest City as a service provider in "Find a Deal" or "PM Rentals" local subforum.

2. **Facebook Groups:**
   - "Cleveland Real Estate Investors"
   - "Northeast Ohio Real Estate Network"
   - "Greater Cleveland REIA"
   - Lurk for 2 weeks, then post a helpful comment with Forest City mention

3. **Local REIA Meetings** — Northeast Ohio Real Estate Investors Association (NOREIA) meets monthly in the Cleveland area. One meeting = 20+ investors in the room.

4. **Wholesaler network** — Wholesalers know every flipper in the market. One relationship with a Cleveland wholesaler = referrals to 10+ investors.

## Referral Structure for Investors

Instead of the $50/referral flat rate, offer investors:

**Option A:** 15% discount on all their properties (volume incentive)
**Option B:** Priority scheduling (within 48 hours guaranteed)
**Option C:** Net-30 invoicing for flippers on active projects (cash flow help)

These matter more to investors than $50 cash referral payments.
