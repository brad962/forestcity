## The Gap This Fills

The contractor conversation tree (`outputs/vera/contractor_conversation_tree_2026-05-18.md`) gets you to YES.  
This file covers everything AFTER the YES — how to confirm the deal, onboard them, and keep referrals flowing.

---

## STEP 1 — Confirmation Text (Send within 1 hour of "YES")

> Perfect. Here's how it works: when your customers mention they need the house or driveway cleaned, just text me their name and number. I'll take it from there. If we close the job, I send you $50. And whenever my customers need [landscaping / roofing / gutters / their service], I send them straight to you. That's it — no forms, no contracts.
>
> What's the best number for me to send you customer referrals?

**Why it works:** Confirms the deal instantly, explains the mechanic in one breath, and gets an immediate action (their referral number). No ambiguity.

---

## STEP 2 — Follow-Up Text (Send 2 hours later or same day)

> One more thing — if you ever have a customer mention dirty siding, stained driveway, algae on the roof, or just a house that needs a clean-up, that's us. We handle the whole exterior. Happy to take a look for free and give them a quote. We make you look good to your customers.
>
> I'll do the same for you — anytime someone asks me about [their service], you're the first name I give.

## STEP 4 — First Referral Ping (Send 1 week later if no referrals yet)

> Hey [Name] — Bradley from Forest City. Quick check-in. Any customers come up recently that needed exterior cleaning? Even if they just mentioned it in passing, I'm happy to reach out. No pressure — just want to make sure I'm top of mind when it comes up.

## STEP 5 — First Referral Closed — Pay and Thank

When they send a referral and you close the job:

> [Name]! Closed that job from [customer name]. Here's your $50 — [Venmo / Zelle / cash your method]. Thank you. Keep 'em coming. I'll do the same for you.

---

## Objections and Responses

**"I don't really send referrals / I don't know if my customers would need that"**
> Totally get it — no pressure. Just save my number. If it ever comes up naturally, you'll know who to call. And I'll do the same for you.

**"I already have someone I use for that"**
> No problem. If they ever let you down or you need a second option, I'm here. I'll still send landscaping / roofing customers your way regardless.

**"What if the customer doesn't go with you?"**
> Then you still tried to help your customer — that's all I can ask. The $50 is only when we close it, so there's no risk on your end.

**"Can I get a business card or something?"**
> Absolutely — I'll drop some off next time I'm in your area. In the meantime, just text people my number: [Bradley's number]. They can text or call.
