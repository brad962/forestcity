## Step 1 — Pull Your Call List (5 min, 8:45am)

Open Mixmax → Property Manager Outreach sequence → sort by **Opens** descending.

Build your list in this priority order:

| Priority | Criteria | Action |
|----------|----------|--------|
| 🔴 **#1** | Opened Touch 3 + opened 2+ earlier emails | Call first |
| 🟠 **#2** | Opened 3+ emails total (any touches) | Call second |
| 🟡 **#3** | Clicked a link in any email | Call third |
| 🟢 **#4** | 2 opens, no click | Call if time allows |

Write down your top 5 names, companies, and phone numbers in your Notes app before you start calling.

---

## Step 2 — Live Call Script

**When they pick up:**

> "Hey [first name], this is Bradley with Forest City Power Washing — I reached out by email a couple weeks ago about keeping up your properties through the summer. Do you have 2 minutes?"

**If YES:**

> "Great. I work with property managers across [their county] — we do exterior cleaning, soft wash roof treatments, driveway and parking lot cleaning. A lot of managers use us before tenant turnover or before annual inspections. Does that sound like something you deal with?

[Listen. If yes:]

> "Perfect — I'd love to walk your property and give you a number. I'm booking into June right now. Would [day] or [day] work for a quick 20-minute site visit?"

**If they ask for pricing up front:**

> "Honestly it really depends on square footage and surface type — I'd rather walk it than guess wrong and waste your time. Most commercial properties in your range are between $X and $Y. Site visits are free and take 20 minutes."

*(Use NE Ohio pricing reference: outputs/tommy/quote_to_close_kit_2026-05-20.md)*

## Step 3 — Voicemail Script (if no answer)

> "Hey [first name], Bradley with Forest City Power Washing — I emailed you a couple times about exterior cleaning for your properties. Giving you a quick call to follow up. We're booking into June right now and I'd love to get you on the schedule. Give me a call back at [Bradley's number] or just reply to my email. Thanks."

**Wait 90 minutes, then send a text:**

> "Hey [first name] — Bradley at Forest City Power Washing. Left you a voicemail. We do exterior cleaning for property managers across [county] — house/siding, driveways, parking lots. If it's worth a 2-min call I'd love to connect. [Bradley's number]"

---

## Step 4 — Text-Only Option (if no direct number)

Look up the company's main number in Google Maps or their website. Call the front desk and ask to speak with [first name].

If you can't get through, send a LinkedIn message instead (they're already connected from yesterday's Touch 3 protocol):

> "Hey [first name] — following up on my emails about exterior cleaning for your properties. Happy to do a quick 20-min site visit with no pressure. Are you the right person to talk to about this, or is there someone else on your team?"

---

## Step 5 — Track Every Call

After each call, update `pipeline_data.json` or your notes:

| Status | What to log |
|--------|-------------|
| Reached, interested | stage → "Estimate Sent", add scheduled date |
| Reached, not now | stage → "Follow-Up", next_followup → June 15 |
| Reached, wrong person | Ask for right contact, note it |
| VM left | note "VM 5/23", follow up text sent |
| No answer | note "No answer 5/23", try again tomorrow |
