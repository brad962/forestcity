# Touch 3 Open Trigger Protocol
### May 21–23, 2026 — The 48-Hour Window
*Tommy Greer | Content & Copy Writer*

---

## THE SITUATION

Touch 3 fires May 22. You have 45 enrolled property managers.  
42% have already opened Touches 1 or 2.  
The 48 hours AFTER Touch 3 drops is the hottest window of the entire sequence.

**This protocol is for that exact window.** When someone opens, you act within 2 hours.

---

## STEP 1 — MONITOR FOR OPENS

**How to check:** Open Mixmax → click your Property Manager sequence → sort by "Last Opened."

**Check at:**
- Thursday morning (8am) — immediately after you wake up
- Thursday noon (12pm) — before lunch
- Thursday 5pm — end of work day
- Friday 9am — any late openers from Thursday evening

**What you're looking for:** Anyone who opened Touch 3 (Stage 3) for the first time. These people read your final email while it was fresh in their minds.

---

## STEP 2 — WHEN SOMEONE OPENS (within 2 hours)

**A) Check LinkedIn first (2 min)**
1. Open Mixmax → find the contact → click their name
2. Look for LinkedIn URL in the contact record
3. If you don't have it: search LinkedIn manually → [First Name] [Last Name] [Company]

**B) Send LinkedIn connection request (3 min)**
Use this template (personalized by trade):

> "Hi [Name], I reached out about power washing for [Company] recently. Wanted to connect here too — I work with property managers across NE Ohio and sometimes the best conversations happen on LinkedIn. Hope to chat soon!"

*Keep it simple — no pitch in the connection request.*

**C) Log it**
In the dashboard → find their contact → update Notes:
> "Opened Touch 3 on [date] — LinkedIn connect sent [date]"

---

## STEP 3 — IF THEY REPLY TO TOUCH 3

You have a full reply kit ready: `outputs/tommy/touch3_reply_response_templates_2026-05-20.md`

**Key rule:** Reply within 2 hours of any reply. If they reply Thursday morning, reply before noon. The quote-to-close kit is `outputs/tommy/quote_to_close_kit_2026-05-20.md`.

---

## STEP 4 — NO REPLY BY THURSDAY NOON

If nobody replies by noon Thursday, start calling. Phone script: `outputs/tommy/hot_lead_phone_script_2026-05-22.md`

**Call order (highest engagement first):**
1. Any contact who opened Touch 3 AND had 3+ opens total
2. Any contact who opened Touch 3 AND had 2 opens total  
3. Any contact who opened Touch 3 for the first time (single opener — less warm but worth a call)

**Voicemail script (15 seconds):**
> "Hey [Name], this is Bradley from Forest City Power Washing. I just sent you a final note about power washing for [Company] and wanted to put a real voice to the email. We're booking into June and I'd love to get you on the schedule. Call or text me at [your number]. Have a great Memorial Day weekend."

**Memorial Day SMS follow-up (send 1 hour after voicemail):**
> "Hey [Name] — Bradley from Forest City Power Washing. Left you a quick voicemail. Happy to chat before the holiday weekend or whenever works for you. Have a great Memorial Day! 🇺🇸"

---

## STEP 5 — FRIDAY (MAY 23)

If you called Thursday and got voicemail on all of them — don't follow up Friday. Most people are already in holiday mode. Let it breathe over the weekend.

**Plan for next contact: Tuesday May 26.**  
Use `outputs/donna/may26_outreach_blitz_brief_2026-05-20.md` — the full post-holiday blitz plan.
