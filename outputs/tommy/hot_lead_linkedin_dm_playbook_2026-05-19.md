## Why This Works

They opened the email twice. They know the name Forest City. A LinkedIn connection request from a real person at the same company lands differently than email #3 — it signals this is a real local business, not a blast campaign.

The sequence: LinkedIn connect → Touch 3 lands the same day or next day → they recognize the name and reply.

## STEP 2 — DM if They Accept (send within 24 hours)

**Template A — Property Managers:**

> Hey [First Name], thanks for connecting. I know you've probably got a list of vendors a mile long, but I wanted to reach out personally.
>
> We've been doing exterior cleaning for property management companies in [their county] — driveways, walkways, building exteriors. Most of our PM clients are on an annual or semi-annual schedule because the HOA / city pressure doesn't go away.
>
> Would it make sense to jump on a 10-minute call sometime this week? Happy to put together a quick quote for whatever properties you manage.
>
> — Bradley

**Template B — Realtors:**

> Lot of agents we work with like having us on call — it's a value-add they can offer sellers without any extra cost to them.

>
> — Bradley
