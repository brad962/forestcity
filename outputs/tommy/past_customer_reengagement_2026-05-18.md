## Why It Works

- "Last year" is a natural, non-pushy reason to reach out
- Specific: "north side," "algae," "mildew" — Marcus's VOC shows customers respond to specifics
- Soft CTA: "just reply with your address" has zero friction
- P.S. removes pressure — this is what actually gets replies from people who DON'T need it
