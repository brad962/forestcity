## SCRIPT 1 — House Washing (most common past service)

Use when: Customer had their house, siding, or exterior washed in 2025 or earlier in 2026.

```
Hey [First Name]! It's Bradley from Forest City Power Washing. Spring's here and the algae is back on a lot of houses we're seeing in [their city]. Want to get yours cleaned up before summer kicks in? I can get you on the schedule this month. — Bradley
```

**Personalize:** Replace [First Name] with their actual name. Replace [their city] with wherever their house is (Strongsville, Parma, Akron, etc.).

**Response handling:**
- "Yes / how much?" → "For a house your size I'd usually be around $[X]. Want me to swing by for a quick look first?" (Schedule estimate)
- "Not this year" → "No worries at all. I'll check back with you in the fall!"
- No response after 48h → Send Script 5 below (neighbor referral pivot)

## SCRIPT 2 — Roof Soft Wash

Use when: Customer had their roof washed (or brought it up). Roof typically needs a refresh every 1-2 years.

```
Hey [First Name]! Bradley from Forest City. The black streaks are starting to come back on a lot of roofs in [their area] — makes sense with the NE Ohio humidity. Yours still looking good from last time, or want to freshen it up? — Bradley
```

**Personalize:** This opens with a "you might need it" not a hard sell — low pressure, high conversion.

**Response handling:**
- "Actually yeah it's looking bad again" → "I can get a price for you over the phone if you send me a photo of the front and back of the roof — usually $[X-X] for a house your size."
- "Looks fine still" → "Good to hear! I'll check back in the fall."
- "Can you do the house too?" → "Absolutely — we can bundle both for a better rate."

## SCRIPT 3 — Driveway / Concrete

Use when: Customer had driveway, walkway, or concrete surfaces done previously.

```
Hey [First Name]! It's Bradley from Forest City. We're doing a bunch of driveways in [their city] this month — want to get yours on the list? I can usually fit you in within the next 2 weeks. — Bradley
```

**Note:** "We're doing driveways in your area" creates FOMO + social proof + urgency in one sentence. It also implies you're already nearby = lower cost = more likely to say yes.

## SCRIPT 4 — Annual Plan Upsell (if they haven't signed up yet)

Use when: Customer who books every year but hasn't signed the annual plan.

```
Hey [First Name], Bradley from Forest City. Quick question — would you want us to just handle your house wash every spring automatically? We have an annual plan where we schedule you in March/April each year, you get priority booking, and it's usually $[X] cheaper than booking separately. Just a yes or no — no pressure. — Bradley
```

**Why:** Annual plan = locked recurring revenue. $325-$850/year per customer locked in.
If they say yes: Text back payment details and add them to the annual plan list in Workiz.

---

## SCRIPT 5 — Neighbor Referral (no-response or "not this year" follow-up)

Use when: Customer didn't book for themselves but might know someone who needs it.

```
No worries at all [First Name]! One more thing — do you have any neighbors who've been meaning to get their house or driveway cleaned? I'll knock $50 off your next service for anyone you send my way. — Bradley
```

**Why:** Even non-buyers become referral sources. A "no" on their own service can become a $50 referral that turns into a $600 job. Sends within 24 hours of a "not this year" response.
