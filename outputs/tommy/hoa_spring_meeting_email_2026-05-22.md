## Why This Angle Works Right Now

May–June is HOA annual meeting season in NE Ohio. Boards review maintenance vendors, approve budgets for exterior work, and vote on property appearance standards — all in one meeting. A community manager who just got asked "why does Building C look dirty?" by 12 homeowners at last Tuesday's meeting is a HOT lead for Forest City RIGHT NOW.

**Timing hook:** "HOA annual meeting season" is a specific, credible reason to reach out in May that 99% of power washing emails don't use. It makes the email feel timely instead of random.

**Use these for contacts with titles:** community manager, community association manager, HOA manager, HOA director, association manager — anyone managing HOA-governed properties.
