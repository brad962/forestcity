# Off-Hours Lead Response Kit
### Tommy Greer | 2026-05-26
*For Facebook and Google ad leads that come in evenings, weekends, and when you're on a job*

---

## The Problem

Facebook Lead Gen ads run 24/7. A lead at 9:30pm is just as real as one at 10am.
Research says: contact within 5 minutes = 9× higher conversion. But you can't always call at 9:30pm.

This kit solves the gap: what to say IMMEDIATELY (within 5 min, by text) even when you can't call.

---

## Rule 1: Respond to every lead within 5 minutes — even if it's a text, not a call

You don't have to call at midnight. You DO have to text.

---

## The Instant Off-Hours Text (send within 5 min of getting the notification)

**Evening (after 6pm):**
> Hey [First Name], this is Bradley from Forest City Power Washing — got your quote request. Appreciate you reaching out. I'm wrapping up the day but I'll give you a call first thing tomorrow morning. In the meantime, what's the property address and what are you looking to get cleaned? — Bradley

**Weekend (Sat/Sun):**
> Hey [First Name] — Bradley from Forest City Power Washing. Got your request, thanks! I'm out doing jobs today but want to make sure I get back to you fast. Can you drop me the address and what service you need? I'll put together a number and call you this afternoon (or Monday morning if you'd prefer). — Bradley

**During a job (any time):**
> Hey [First Name] — Bradley at Forest City Power Washing. Got your quote request! I'm mid-job right now but I didn't want to leave you waiting. Can you text me the address and what you want cleaned? I'll call you the moment I'm done — usually within an hour. — Bradley

---

## Rule 2: Set a specific callback time in your text

"I'll call tomorrow" = vague. "I'll call you Monday morning around 9am" = they'll answer.

**Add this to any off-hours text:**
> I'll call you [Monday morning around 9am / this afternoon around 3pm / tomorrow around 10am]. Does that work?

---

## What to Do If They Reply to Your Text

They texted back? Great — answer the quote right there.

**Quick phone quote (for simple residential):**
> House washing in [city]? Probably $[X]–$[Y] depending on size. I'd want to drive by to confirm, but that's the range. Want me to swing by this week for a quick look?

Use the `outputs/tommy/quote_to_close_kit_[date].md` pricing reference for NE Ohio ranges.

## The Morning Callback Script (for leads from the previous evening/weekend)

**Call script when they answer:**
> "Hey [First Name], this is Bradley from Forest City Power Washing — I sent you a text last night when I got your quote request. Thanks for your patience. I wanted to make sure I got back to you fast. Did you get a chance to look at any other companies yet? [Pause for their answer.] Great — what are you looking to get done?"

**If they don't answer:**
> Voicemail: "Hey [First Name], it's Bradley from Forest City Power Washing — I texted you last night after you requested a quote. Just following up by phone. Give me a call back at [number] whenever you're free today. Thanks!"

**Text if they don't answer the call (send immediately after voicemail):**
> Hey [First Name] — left you a voicemail just now. Totally understand if now isn't a good time. Just reply here when you're ready and I'll get you a quote fast. — Bradley / Forest City Power Washing

## How to Set Up the 5-Minute Auto-Response in Meta Business Suite

*(This is a no-code setup that lets Facebook send the first reply automatically — even while you're sleeping)*

1. Go to Meta Business Suite (business.facebook.com)
2. Inbox → Automations → Instant Reply
3. Turn ON for Facebook and Instagram
4. Message to set:
   > "Hey, thanks for reaching out to Forest City Power Washing! We got your message and will reply within a few hours. For fastest service, text us directly at [your number]. — Bradley"
5. Save.

This buys you time while ensuring the lead knows a real person is on the other end.

---

## What NOT to Do

- ❌ Don't call at 10pm unless the lead specifically texted "call me now"
- ❌ Don't wait until "tomorrow morning" without sending an immediate acknowledgment text
- ❌ Don't use generic "we'll be in touch soon" — leads disappear with vague timing
- ❌ Don't let a weekend lead sit until Monday without a text acknowledgment on Saturday
