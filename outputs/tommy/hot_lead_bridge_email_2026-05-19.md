- This is NOT a follow-up to a sequence. Don't reference "my previous email" or "I've tried to reach you."

## AFTER SOMEONE REPLIES

If they reply with interest:
1. Move them to "Replied" in the pipeline
2. Respond within 2 hours (or same day)
3. Ask: "What's your biggest headache right now with your exterior surfaces?"
4. From there → estimate or site visit

If they say "not interested" / "we have a vendor":
1. Move to "Closed Lost"
2. Reply: "Totally understand. Mind if I follow up in the fall? A lot of property managers like to shop after summer season winds down." — keeps the door open.
