# Pre-Job Reminder Text Sequence
### Tommy Greer | Copy & Scripts | 2026-05-24
*Send before every confirmed job. Reduces no-shows, sets expectations, signals professionalism.*

---

## Why This Matters
Facebook and Google ad leads are NEW customers — they've never seen Bradley work. A confirmation text the day before + morning-of text:
1. Reduces no-shows (biggest cost = driving to an empty house)
2. Sets expectations for soft-wash smell and residue (prevents "what did you spray on my house?" calls)
3. Makes Forest City look professional before crew even arrives
4. Creates a touchpoint for the annual plan pitch at job completion

---

## TEXT 1 — Day Before (send the evening before the scheduled job, 5–7pm)

**Standard version:**
> "Hey [FirstName], this is Bradley from Forest City Power Washing. Just confirming we're scheduled for tomorrow, [Day] [Date], starting around [TIME]. We'll be there for approximately [DURATION].
>
> Quick heads up: we use a soft-wash solution that may have a mild bleach smell for a few hours — totally normal and safe for plants and pets once dry (usually 30 min). No need to be home unless you want to be.
>
> Any questions, just reply or call [PHONE]. See you tomorrow!"

**Shorter version (for return customers):**
> "Hey [FirstName], Bradley from Forest City. See you tomorrow! We're on for [TIME]. Soft-wash solution will smell briefly but clears up fast. Text me if anything changes."

---

## TEXT 2 — Day Of, Morning (send 30–60 min before arrival)

**Standard version:**
> "Hey [FirstName], Bradley from Forest City. We're about [X] minutes away — on track for [TIME]. If you have any specific areas you want us to focus on, reply now and I'll let the crew know. See you soon!"

**Running late (send if delayed > 20 min):**
> "Hey [FirstName], quick heads up — we're running about [X] minutes behind. Still coming, ETA [NEW TIME]. Appreciate your patience!"

**Gate/access reminder (for properties with gates or access codes):**
> "Hey [FirstName], heading your way shortly. Can you confirm the gate code / make sure [specific access point] is unlocked? Thanks!"

---

## TEXT 3 — Job Complete (send within 30 min of finishing)

**Standard version:**
> "Hey [FirstName], we just finished up! The [driveway/house/roof/deck] is all cleaned and looking great. Take a look when you get a chance — I think you'll be happy with it.
>
> If you notice anything we missed or have any questions, text me directly. Thanks for having us out!"

**With photo prompt (if before/after photos were taken):**
> "Hey [FirstName], all done! I'll send over a before/after photo shortly so you can see the difference. If you love the results, a Google review would mean a lot — [Google review link]. Thanks!"

---

## Soft-Wash FAQ for Customers Who Ask (reply template)

**"Is it safe for my plants/garden?"**
> "Yes — we pre-wet plants and rinse them after. The solution biodegrades quickly and won't harm established plants. If you have anything fragile, let me know when we arrive."

**"Do I need to be home?"**
> "Nope! We just need access to the area. If there's a gate or specific instructions, just leave a note or text me. We'll lock up / close everything behind us."

**"How long until I can use my driveway/deck?"**
> "Driveway: 20–30 minutes to dry. Deck: about an hour to be safe if it's wet, but you can walk on it immediately."

**"Will the algae come back?"**
> "Usually takes 1–2 years for algae to return, depending on tree coverage and shade. Our annual plan locks you in for a spring + fall wash so it never gets bad again."

---

## Logistics
- Add to Workiz notes when confirmation texts are sent
- If no response to Day Before text by 9pm → send a follow-up: "Just confirming you got my text — we're still on for tomorrow. Reply if anything changes."
- If unreachable by morning of job → try calling before driving out
