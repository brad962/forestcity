## Recommended Test for Round 2

**Start with Pair A.** The pain/anticipation version ("Before the tenants complain...") is proven in home services outreach. Property managers are trained to anticipate tenant complaints — this hits that exact nerve.

**If Pair A shows <30% improvement in opens after week 1**, switch to Pair D ("Scheduling for June"). Ultra-short subjects consistently beat over-personalized ones in cold commercial sequences when the sender is unknown.
