**Page slug:** /roof-soft-wash

**H1 (swap into web builder exactly):**
Your Roof Looks Black from the Street. That's Not Age — It's Alive.

**Subheadline:**
Gloeocapsa magma — a bacteria — is eating your shingles. We kill it. One treatment. Results that last 3–5 years.

**Body:**

If your roof has dark streaks or black patches running down from the ridge, you have a bacteria problem. It's called Gloeocapsa magma, and it feeds on the limestone filler in asphalt shingles. In Northeast Ohio, the lake moisture makes it grow faster than almost anywhere else in the country.

Left alone, it shortens your roof's life by 5–10 years. A new roof is $15,000. We're not that.

Pressure washing won't fix it — it knocks off the granules and the bacteria grows back in 6 months. Soft wash roof cleaning uses a low-pressure application of a solution that kills the bacteria at the root. The streaks rinse off. The bacteria is dead. Your roof looks 10 years newer.

**Who books this:**
- Homeowners whose roof looks black from the street and they're embarrassed about it
- Anyone thinking about selling — buyers don't want to look at a black roof at $400K
- Property managers with multi-unit buildings — we do volume pricing
- Homeowners who want to extend their roof's life without a $15,000 replacement

**What we use:**
Sodium hypochlorite solution applied at low pressure — no shingle damage, and we protect your landscaping before we start.

**Pricing:**
Most residential roofs run $350–$600 depending on pitch and size. Free quote, usually same-week. We give you a before/after photo set when we're done.
