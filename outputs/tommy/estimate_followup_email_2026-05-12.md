## Copy Notes for Bradley

**What makes these work:**
1. **Short.** Nobody reads long emails from a home services company. Every template is under 100 words.
2. **Personal.** Signed from Bradley, not "the Forest City team." People book people.
3. **Scarcity is real.** Spring genuinely fills up — use it. Don't fake urgency that isn't there.
4. **The 30-day honor window** on the last follow-up — this reduces the friction of the "not right now" situation and keeps the lead warm.
5. **Text follow-ups** convert better than email for most home service leads — add the cell number to your workflow.
