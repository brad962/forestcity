## The Play
Send within **2 hours of job completion** while the customer is still looking at a clean house and satisfaction is at peak. One text, one email, locks in $400–$600/year recurring revenue and eliminates the re-acquisition problem.

**Annual Plan:** Two visits/year — spring wash + pre-winter wash. Discounted rate vs. booking each individually.

**Pricing:**
- Individual visits: ~$250–$450 each
- Annual Plan: $400–$600/year (saves them $100–$300, locks in 2 recurring jobs for you)

## Objection Handling

**"I'll just call when I need it."**
> "Totally — and you can always do that. The main thing you're getting with the plan is the price lock and priority scheduling. Peak season (May–June) we're usually booked 2–3 weeks out. Annual plan customers get first pick. But if you'd rather book as-needed, that works too — just want to make sure you know the option's there."

**"How much does it cost exactly?"**
> "It's [PRICE]/year — that covers both visits. If you booked each one separately, you'd probably pay $[individual visit price x 2]. So you're saving around $[savings] and not having to remember to call in spring when everyone else is calling."

**"We might be moving."**
> "Totally fair — no contract, so if you move before the second visit, we'd just prorate the difference or do a one-time rate for the visit. No risk on your end."

**"Let me think about it."**
> "Of course. I'll send you a quick follow-up in a few weeks if that's OK. No pressure either way."
