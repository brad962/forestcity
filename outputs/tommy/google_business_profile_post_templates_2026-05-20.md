## Why GBP Posts Matter

Google Business Profile posts appear directly in Google Maps and local search results when someone searches "power washing near me" or "house washing Cleveland." They cost nothing. They take 3 minutes to post. They signal to Google that your business is active, which improves your local ranking.

**Goal:** One post per week during peak season (May–August).
**Where:** Google Business Profile → Add Update → What's New or Offer
**Length:** 100–300 words max. Google truncates longer posts.
**Photo:** Always include. Job photo preferred. Logo if no job photo available.
