# Google Review Request Sequence
### Tommy Greer | Content & Copy | 2026-05-18
*Used after every completed power washing job*

---

## Why This Matters

Every 5-star Google review is worth roughly $200–$500 in future jobs.
Cleveland homeowners Google "power washing near me" every day in May and June.
The companies with 20+ recent reviews win that traffic. Forest City needs to be one of them.

Send this sequence within 24 hours of every job completion.

---

## Touch 1 — Text (same day or next morning, 9–11am)

**Send from Bradley's personal number.**

> Hey [First Name]! Bradley from Forest City Power Washing — hope the house is looking exactly how you wanted it. If we earned it, would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? Takes 2 minutes and means the world to a small local business. [GOOGLE REVIEW LINK]

**Notes:**
- Keep [GOOGLE REVIEW LINK] as your Google Business Profile review shortlink.
- Get your shortlink: Google Business Profile → Home → "Get more reviews" → copy the link.
- Send within 4 hours of job completion while the before/after transformation is still fresh.

---

## Touch 2 — Email (3 days later, if no review left)

**Subject:** One quick favor from Forest City Power Washing

> Hi [First Name],
>
> Bradley here — just checking in after your recent power washing job. Hope everything looks great.
>
> I have one small favor to ask: would you take 2 minutes to leave us a Google review? For a small local business in Northeast Ohio, reviews are everything. They help us get found by homeowners in your neighborhood who need the same work done.
>
> Here's the direct link — just click, give us stars, and say what you thought:
> → [GOOGLE REVIEW LINK]
>
> Doesn't have to be long. Even just "great job, house looks clean" goes a long way.
>
> Thanks for trusting us with your home.
>
> — Bradley
> Forest City Power Washing
> Cleveland, OH

---

## Touch 3 — Text follow-up (7 days later, only if job was $500+)

> Hey [First Name]! Bradley again — just making sure everything is still looking good after last week. Any chance you had a moment to drop us that Google review? We'd really appreciate it. [GOOGLE REVIEW LINK] 🙏

---

## What to Say When They Ask "What Should I Write?"

If a customer says "sure, what should I say?" — give them this prompt:

> "Just mention what you had done, what it looked like before and after, and whether you'd recommend us. Totally in your own words!"

**Never write reviews for customers or suggest specific language** — Google flags it.
