## SCRIPT A — When They Pick Up (Live Answer)

**Opening (5 seconds — natural, not salesy):**

> "Hey [first name], this is Bradley with Forest City Power Washing — do you have just a quick minute?"

*Wait for them to say yes or ask who you are.*

**If they say "sure" / "what's this about":**

> "I reached out a couple weeks ago about exterior cleaning for your properties — I know you've probably got a dozen emails a day, so I just wanted to put a real voice to the name. We do power washing and soft wash roof cleaning for property managers all over [Cuyahoga / Lake / Lorain] County.
>
> Quick question — do you have any properties where the exterior needs attention going into summer? Whether that's algae on the siding, a dirty parking lot, anything like that?"

**Let them talk.** Don't pitch further until they've answered.

**If they say YES / have a property in mind:**
> "Perfect — what kind of property is it, single location or do you manage a portfolio?"
> *→ Get: property type, number of locations, general location*
>
> "Got it. I can get you a quote — usually takes me about 10 minutes to walk the property and I can have a number to you same day. What's a good time this week?"

**If they say "send me info" / "email me":**
> "Happy to. What email should I send to — same one you're on now?"
> *→ Confirm email. Then:*
> "I'll send over a one-pager. One thing I'd include is a before/after photo from a commercial property we did in [nearby city] — similar size. I'll get that over to you today."

**If they say "not right now" / "we have someone":**
> "No problem at all. Do you have someone on a contract, or is it more on-call as you need it?"
> *→ If on contract: "When does that renewal come up? I'd love to give you a second quote — we're usually 10–15% under what the big regional companies charge."*
> *→ If on-call: "Got it. Whenever you need a quote, I'm usually same-week available. Can I text you my number so it's handy?"*

**Closing:**
> "Appreciate the time. I'll follow up [Tuesday] if I don't hear from you. Enjoy the long weekend."

## SCRIPT B — Voicemail (15 Seconds Max)

> "Hey [first name], this is Bradley from Forest City Power Washing — I reached out by email a couple weeks ago. Just wanted to put a real voice to the name. We do exterior cleaning for property managers across NE Ohio and I'd love to talk about your properties for maybe 5 minutes. Give me a call back at [your number] whenever works — or just reply to my email. Hope you have a good Memorial Day weekend."

**Key rules for voicemail:**
- Say your name twice (once at start, once at end if you have time)
- Say "property managers" — they need to hear their role, not just "power washing company"
- Give them an easy out ("reply to my email") — removes commitment anxiety
- Keep it warm, not scripted

## SCRIPT C — Text/SMS After Voicemail (Send Same Day)

> "Hey [first name] — Bradley from Forest City Power Washing. Left you a voicemail. No pressure at all — just let me know if exterior cleaning for your properties is ever on your radar and I'll get you a fast quote. Happy to start with just one property."
