# Annual Plan Customer Welcome Kit
*Tommy Greer | Content & Copy Writer*
*Written: 2026-05-24 | Use after every annual plan YES*

**When:** Send Touch 1 within 30 minutes of customer agreeing to the annual plan.
**Context:** Annual Plan = 2 visits/year ($325–$550 depending on property size). Customer just said YES.
**Goal:** Lock in the relationship, schedule both visits, plant the referral seed.

---

## TOUCH 1 — Confirmation Text (within 30 min of YES)

Send from Bradley's personal phone or Twilio number.

---

**Text:**

Hey [first name] — confirmed! You're on the Forest City Annual Plan.

Here's what that means: we'll reach out in a few weeks to schedule your first visit (spring/summer), and we'll come back in September or October before the cold hits.

I'll send a quick email with the details. Thanks for having us!

— Bradley, Forest City Power Washing

---

**Notes:**
- Use their first name — this is personal, not corporate
- Keep it under 160 characters to avoid splitting into 2 texts
- If it's August–September: flip the order ("first visit this fall, second visit next spring")

---

## TOUCH 2 — Welcome Email (same day, within 3 hours)

Subject: Welcome to the Forest City Annual Plan — here's what to expect

---

Hey [first name],

Welcome to the Forest City Annual Plan — really glad you're in.

Here's the simple version of what you get:

**Visit 1 (Spring/Summer):** We do a full exterior clean — house, driveway, whatever's on your plan. Usually takes 2–3 hours. We work around your schedule.

**Visit 2 (Fall, before the cold):** Second visit to clear out the summer algae buildup and anything the season left behind. Keeps the house protected going into winter.

**What you don't have to do:** Call us every year and remember to book. We'll reach out to you before each season. You just say "yep" and we're on the schedule.

**Pricing:** You locked in $[PRICE]/year at today's rate. Your rate won't change as long as you stay on the plan.

I'll be in touch to schedule your first visit. Typically I reach out 1–2 weeks before I'm running jobs in your area.

Any questions before then — just reply to this or text me: [Bradley's phone].

Thanks again,

Bradley
Forest City Power Washing
[Phone]
forestcitypowerwashing.com

---

**Email notes:**
- Fill in PRICE from the quote ($325, $400, $450, $550 depending on scope)
- If visit 1 is already scheduled, include the date/time
- Keep it plain text — no fancy template, no logo header — feels more personal

---

## TOUCH 3 — Schedule Your Visits Email (within 48 hours)

Subject: Let's lock in your dates for the year

---

Hey [first name],

Quick follow-up — ready to get your two visits on the calendar.

For your **first visit**, we're currently booking [MONTH, e.g. "mid-June"]. I can put you down for:
- [Option A: date and time]
- [Option B: date and time]

Just reply with which works or give me a different window and I'll fit you in.

**Second visit** we'll schedule in late August/September when we get closer — I'll reach out then.

Talk soon,

Bradley

---

**Notes:**
- Fill in specific dates before sending — not just "mid-June"
- If you're more than 2 weeks out on scheduling, skip this and send it closer to your open slots
- This is the step most businesses skip — customers love feeling like they're actually on a calendar

---

## TOUCH 4 — Referral Seed Text (2 weeks after first visit)

Send ~14 days after the first job is completed. Timing: when satisfaction is fresh but the novelty hasn't worn off.

---

**Text:**

Hey [first name], hope everything still looks good out there! Quick question — if you know anyone in [neighborhood/city] who could use a wash, we'd love the intro. We'll take care of them the same way we take care of you, and there's a $50 credit on your account for every referral that books.

Just pass along our number or have them text us. Thanks!

— Bradley, Forest City

---

**Notes:**
- This gets a 15–25% referral rate from satisfied annual plan customers — do not skip it
- "$50 credit" can be applied to their next visit or paid as cash/Venmo — your choice
- If the customer seems like a real enthusiast, ask them to leave a Google review at the same time

## FAQ — Answers for Common Annual Plan Questions

**Q: What if I want to cancel?**
"No contracts. If you decide you don't want the fall visit, just let me know and I'll pro-rate what you've paid. No hard feelings."

**Q: What if I move?**
"We can transfer the plan to your new address if we service that area, or apply the remaining balance toward a one-time clean for the new place. Just let us know."

**Q: Does the price go up next year?**
"Your rate is locked in as long as you stay active on the plan. We don't raise rates on existing customers."

**Q: What does the plan cover?**
"Whatever we agreed on when you signed up — [house wash, driveway, etc.]. If you want to add something at your second visit, just ask when we're there and we'll quote it on the spot."

**Q: Do I have to be home?**
"Nope. As long as we can access the exterior of the property, you don't need to be home. We'll text you when we arrive and when we're done."
