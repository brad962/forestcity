## OPTION A — Social Proof / Specificity Angle

**Subject:** 3 properties on Haverford Ave looked like this last week

**Email body:**

Hi {{firstName}},

I cleaned 3 properties on Haverford Ave in Shaker Heights last Thursday. Two of them had been waiting on bids for months. Both booked the same day they replied.

I'm the owner. I do the work. No crews, no subcontractors — just me and a soft wash system that doesn't damage surfaces.

I serve {{county}} County and I have a few spots left before June.

Mind if I send you a ballpark for {{company}}? Takes 2 minutes — just the address and what you want cleaned.

Bradley Neal
Forest City Power Washing
(440) 867-7068

---

**Why this works:** Real specificity (street, city, days ago) triggers credibility. "I'm the owner. I do the work." answers the #1 unspoken PM objection before it comes up. Short close — asking for an address, not a commitment.

## OPTION B — Peer/Fear Angle (Seasonal Urgency)

**Subject:** The algae is back. Your tenants are noticing.

**Email body:**

Hi {{firstName}},

Spring in Northeast Ohio means one thing for exterior surfaces: the algae is back.

Property managers who didn't deal with it in April are starting to hear about it from tenants in June. By August, it's a maintenance ticket, a lease-renewal conversation, and a photo in a Google review.

I'm Bradley Neal, owner of Forest City Power Washing. I serve {{county}} County and I'm booking June now.

Takes about 2 hours per property. Soft wash — safe for all surfaces, gutters stay clear. No pressure damage.

Can I put a quote together for you? Just reply with the property address.

Bradley
(440) 867-7068

---

**Why this works:** "Your tenants are noticing" puts the PM in their tenant's shoes — their job is to prevent tenant complaints. The escalation path (maintenance ticket → lease renewal → Google review) makes the cost of inaction feel real without being pushy. Clear close.

---

## OPTION C — Direct / No-Fluff Angle

**Subject:** Quick question about {{company}}'s properties

**Email body:**

Hi {{firstName}},

Do you already have a vendor for exterior washing at {{company}}?

If yes — no problem. If not, I'm the owner of Forest City Power Washing and I cover Northeast Ohio. I work with property managers to keep buildings clean between major contracts.

Most properties I work on are done in a morning. No crews, just me. Soft wash system — safe for vinyl, stucco, EIFS, brick.

If you're open to it, I can send a quote with just the property address. Takes 5 minutes on my end.

Bradley Neal
Forest City Power Washing
(440) 867-7068

---

**Why this works:** Opens with a direct question — forces a mental answer ("do we have someone?"). Acknowledges if they do ("no problem"), so they don't feel sold to. The "no crews, just me" line lands differently in round 2 because it's unexpected from a vendor who's already emailed 3 times.
