## Facebook Ad Variations (5)

---

### Ad 1 — Before/After

**Headline (39 chars):** Looks Better Than the Day You Bought It

**Primary Text:**
"Looks better than when we bought it." That's a direct quote from a customer in Strongsville last week. We cleaned the siding and the driveway — they couldn't believe it was the same house. What does yours look like right now?

**CTA Label:** Get a Free Quote

**Creative Direction:**
Side-by-side before/after carousel. Frame 1: weathered siding with visible green algae and a stained driveway — shot from the curb, full-house view. Frame 2: same angle, same house, after — bright, clean, almost looks freshly painted. No text overlays on the main frames. On the final card: "Forest City Power Washing — Northeast Ohio." Let the transformation speak. Works best boosted to homeowners 40–60 in Cuyahoga and Summit counties.

### Ad 2 — Urgency / Seasonal

**Headline (28 chars):** Fourth of July Is 19 Days Out

**Primary Text:**
We hear it every year: "I just want it done before the Fourth." July 4th weekend slots are already going. We serve 7 counties in Northeast Ohio — Cuyahoga, Lake, Lorain, Geauga, Medina, Summit, Portage. Hit the link and get a quote while we've still got your weekend open.

**CTA Label:** Book Now

**Creative Direction:**
Single image. Clean, bright exterior of a suburban home — summer light, American flag in the yard, fresh-cut lawn. The house looks perfect. Text badge overlay in the upper corner: "July 4 Slots Filling Fast." No clutter. The image is the aspiration; the badge is the close. Run this from now through June 28, then retire it.

### Ad 3 — Social Proof

**Headline (35 chars):** We Were the Only Ones Who Called Back

**Primary Text:**
"I asked three companies for a quote. Only one called me back." That was us. Forest City Power Washing — insured, reliable, and we actually answer the phone. Book a quote and find out why NE Ohio homeowners keep telling their neighbors about us.

**CTA Label:** Get a Free Quote

**Creative Direction:**
Clean testimonial-style layout. White card design with the pull-quote front and center in large text — "Only one called me back." Below it: the Forest City logo and the service area. Optional background: a clean, bright front exterior. Works as a retargeting ad for website visitors who filled out a form or visited the quote page but didn't convert.

---

### Ad 4 — Problem / Solution

**Headline (38 chars):** If You Can Smell the North Side, It's Bad

**Primary Text:**
One of our customers said it best: "I could smell the mold on the north side of the house." That's not just a curb appeal problem — that's water damage waiting to happen. Soft washing kills mold and algae at the source. One job, gone.

**CTA Label:** Learn More

**Creative Direction:**
Close-up shot of dark, visibly damp north-facing siding — the kind with the greenish-black mold line near the foundation. Gritty but not gross. Text overlay on the left side: "That smell isn't going away on its own." Second frame (carousel or swipe): the same section of siding, clean and dry. Best format for cold awareness audiences — this stops the scroll with the problem before selling the solution.

---

### Ad 5 — Offer

**Headline (31 chars):** Curb Appeal Package — Book June

**Primary Text:**
House washing + driveway cleaning + one extra surface, starting at $299. We're Forest City Power Washing — covering all 7 Northeast Ohio counties. June is busy and we're filling up. Get your quote now before the Fourth.

**CTA Label:** Get a Free Quote

**Creative Direction:**
Bright, clean full-house exterior — big curb appeal energy. Small badge in the corner: "From $299 · 7 NE Ohio Counties." Forest City logo at the bottom. This is the conversion ad — pair it with the Lead Gen campaign. Targeting: homeowners 35–65, home improvement/real estate interests, existing website retargeting pool. Don't run alongside the urgency ad (Ad 2) in the same ad set — let one message dominate.
