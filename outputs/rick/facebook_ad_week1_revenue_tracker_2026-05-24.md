## DECISION TREE

```
CTR < 0.5% for 3+ days → Swap the creative (new image or new first-line hook)
Cost per lead > $50 for 3+ days → Widen audience (add more interests or counties)
0 leads after Day 3 → Test the lead form yourself — submit a fake entry, confirm email arrives
0 leads after Day 5 → Pause. Check: Is the campaign active? Is the budget being spent? Is the audience too narrow?
Leads coming in but 0 bookings → Your response time is too slow (must call within 5 min). Read: outputs/rick/facebook_lead_response_sop_2026-05-24.md
1 ad outperforming all others → Pause the rest. Scale the winner.
Spending $30/day and profitable → Increase to $50/day in Week 3
```
