## Facebook Ad Variations (5)

---

### Ad 1 — Before/After

**Headline (30 chars):** One Job. This Is What Changed.

**Primary Text:**
"I saw your before and after and called the next morning." We hear that a lot. Two hours of work, and the house looks like it did the day they moved in. Book a free quote — we cover all 7 Northeast Ohio counties and most jobs are in and out the same day.

**CTA Label:** Get a Free Quote

**Creative Direction:**
Classic two-frame carousel. Both shots from the same curb-level angle — full front of house. Frame 1: algae-stained siding, yellowed concrete, the kind of photo that makes a homeowner cringe in recognition. Frame 2: same house, crisp and clean, summer light making the siding pop. No text overlays on the image frames — let the transformation speak. Final card: "Forest City Power Washing · Free Quote · 7 NE Ohio Counties." Target homeowners 38–62 in Cuyahoga, Lake, and Summit. This is the primary Awareness campaign ad — straightforward proof, no urgency required.

---

### Ad 2 — Urgency / Seasonal

**Headline (35 chars):** Summer's Half Over. Still See It?

**Primary Text:**
July 4 came and went. If you noticed the house during the cookout — the siding, the driveway, the deck — you're not going to stop noticing it. August goes fast. We're still booking mid-July slots across all 7 Northeast Ohio counties. Book this week before the summer schedule fills out.

**CTA Label:** Book Now

**Creative Direction:**
Single image. Clean, sun-drenched backyard scene — patio, deck, summer vibe — with one deliberately contrasting detail: algae-stained siding visible in the background. The disconnect is the point. Bold text badge upper-right: "July Slots Still Open." Forest City logo bottom-left. Run as the primary Lead Gen ad this week. The emotional hook is subtle regret, not panic — post-holiday, the urgency has shifted from countdown to opportunity. Retire by July 25 or when "August Slots" language becomes more accurate.

**Headline (32 chars):** Every Kid Drop-Off, They See It.

**Primary Text:**
"The kids are home all summer and their friends' parents are dropping them off — I notice it now." It's peak visibility season. Everybody's outside. Your house is on display every day. Forest City Power Washing is insured, actually shows up, and serves 7 counties across Northeast Ohio. Get your quote before the week's booked.

**CTA Label:** Get a Free Quote

**Creative Direction:**
Testimonial-style layout. Pull-quote in large clean type on a white card: "I notice it now." Below it: "— Northeast Ohio homeowner" in smaller italics. Forest City logo and "Insured · Serving 7 NE Ohio Counties" at the bottom. Optional background: a bright, friendly suburban front yard with visible siding. Run this in the Retargeting campaign — people who've engaged with an ad or visited the quote page already know who we are. The social visibility angle closes bottom-of-funnel by adding peer pressure to the trust signals.

### Ad 4 — Problem / Solution

**Headline (29 chars):** You Drive Past It Twice a Day.

**Primary Text:**
"Every morning I pull out of the driveway and I see the streaks — it's been like that for months." Algae doesn't go away on its own. In Northeast Ohio, Lake Erie humidity keeps feeding it year-round. Soft washing kills it at the root — not just knocks it around. One job. A couple of hours. Gone.

**CTA Label:** Learn More

**Creative Direction:**
Close-up shot of a concrete driveway or lower siding — visible algae streaks and dark staining, shot in bright summer morning light that makes the grime look harsh and obvious. Text overlay lower-left in clean white font: "It doesn't clean itself." Second carousel frame: same surface, post-wash, sharp and bright. Run in the Awareness campaign against Ad 1 to split-test hooks — the daily frustration loop vs. the straight before/after. Marcus flagged the "every morning" phrase as a year-round high-conversion angle, especially for homeowners who've been in delay mode since spring.

Smart move booking in July: summer slots are still available, photos come out at their best in the light, and you're not scrambling in August with everyone else. Curb Appeal package from $299. House + driveway + one extra surface. Seven NE Ohio counties.

**CTA Label:** Get a Free Quote

**Creative Direction:**
Bright, polished exterior shot — a listing-ready suburban home in summer light, the kind of photo that looks good on Zillow. Two small text badges: "From $299 · 7 NE Ohio Counties" and "Selling this fall? Don't wait." Forest City logo bottom-right. Target homeowners 40–65 with real estate and home improvement interests. Pair with Lookalike audience if available. September listings are a real buyer segment — Marcus confirmed it in the June VOC. This is a sleeper performer; if it pulls leads, scale budget on it in Week 2.
