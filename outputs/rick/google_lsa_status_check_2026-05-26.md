## What Happens When Approved

1. Google sends you a "Google Guaranteed" badge
2. Your business card appears at the VERY TOP of relevant local searches
3. Callers see your name, rating, and the Google badge before any other result
4. Each valid lead (phone call or message) costs $15–$35
5. You can dispute bad leads (wrong service area, spam) for credit

**NE Ohio power washing benchmarks:**
- Typical CTR on LSA: 20–35% (vs. 5–10% for regular Google Ads)
- Close rate on LSA leads: 40–60% (higher-intent callers)
- Average job value: $300–$600 residential, $800–$3,000 commercial
- Break-even: ~1 job per week covers the ad spend
