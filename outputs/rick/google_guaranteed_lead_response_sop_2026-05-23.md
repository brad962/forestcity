# Google Guaranteed — Lead Response SOP
*Rick Santoro | Forest City Power Washing*
*Created: 2026-05-23 | Required BEFORE Google Guaranteed ads go live*

---

## Why This Matters
Google Guaranteed tracks your response time and booking rate. Slow responders get **fewer leads** automatically — Google redirects lead volume to faster competitors. Target: **respond within 5 minutes** of the lead notification arriving.

---

## Step 1 — Lead Notification Setup

Google will notify you via:
- **Email** to the Google Business Profile email
- **App notification** via Google Local Services Ads app (download before launch)

**Required before launch:**
- [ ] Download Google Local Services Ads app on your phone
- [ ] Turn on push notifications for the app
- [ ] Confirm the notification email is one you check actively

---

## Step 2 — When a Lead Comes In (First 5 Minutes)

### Immediate: Call First
Call the number Google provides **within 5 minutes**. Google logs response time.

**Call Script — Live Answer:**
> "Hi, this is Bradley from Forest City Power Washing — I just got your request. Are you available now to go over what you're looking for?"

*(If they say yes — go to Step 3: Quote Conversation)*

**Call Script — Goes to Voicemail:**
> "Hi [first name], this is Bradley from Forest City Power Washing. I saw your request just come in — I'd love to help. I have availability this week. Give me a call back at [YOUR NUMBER] and we'll get you a free quote. Looking forward to it."

---

## Step 3 — Text Follow-Up (2 Minutes After Voicemail)

Send a text immediately after leaving voicemail:

> "Hi [name] — Bradley from Forest City Power Washing. Just left you a voicemail. Happy to text if that's easier. What's the address and what are you looking to have cleaned?"

---

## Step 4 — Quote Conversation (Live Call)

Ask in this order:
1. **"What are you looking to have cleaned?"** — house wash, driveway, roof, deck?
2. **"Is it a one-story or two-story home?"** — affects pricing
3. **"What's the address?"** — look it up on Google Maps while talking to estimate size
4. **"Are you looking at getting this done soon, or just getting an idea of cost?"** — gauges urgency

**Ballpark pricing (NE Ohio):**
| Service | Range |
|---------|-------|
| House wash (single-story) | $200–$350 |
| House wash (two-story) | $300–$500 |
| Driveway/sidewalk | $100–$200 |
| Roof soft wash | $350–$600 |
| Deck wash | $150–$300 |
| Full package (house + driveway + deck) | $500–$900 |

**Quote delivery:**
> "Based on what you described, I'm looking at somewhere around [range]. I can send you a formal quote by email tonight. What's the best email for you?"

---

## Step 5 — Quote Follow-Up Sequence

| Timeline | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Same evening | Send formal quote via email (use Workiz) |
| +24 hours | Text: "Hey [name] — just checking in on the quote I sent. Any questions?" |
| +4 days | Email follow-up (use Tommy's quote_followup_sequence) |
| +7 days | Final text: "Last check-in — happy to adjust the scope if needed. Just reply here." |

---

## Step 6 — Booking in Workiz

When they say yes:
1. Create job in Workiz — JobType = **"Power Washing"** (required for reports to work)
2. Get preferred date + confirm address
3. Text confirmation: "You're on the schedule for [date/time]. We'll send a reminder the evening before."
4. Same-day text Tommy's annual plan upsell (see: `outputs/tommy/annual_plan_upsell_sequence_2026-05-23.md`)

---

## Step 7 — Booking Rate Matters

Google Guaranteed penalizes low booking rates. If you receive leads and don't book them, Google assumes your service is low quality and cuts lead volume.

**Target booking rate: 40%+ of Google Guaranteed leads**

If you get a bad lead (wrong service area, spam):
- Mark it as "Dispute" in the Google Local Services Ads app immediately — you won't be charged and it doesn't hurt your booking rate
