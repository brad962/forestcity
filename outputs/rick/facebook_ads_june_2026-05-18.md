## Campaign Context

It's May 18. June is 2 weeks away. Peak season in NE Ohio is right now — algae is visible, real estate is moving, homeowners are thinking about curb appeal before summer. These ads create urgency around June booking before it fills.

**Campaign type:** Lead gen form or "DM us" CTA
**Audience:** Homeowners 35–65, Cuyahoga / Lake / Lorain / Summit / Medina counties
**Budget suggestion:** $10–15/day, pause when calendar fills
