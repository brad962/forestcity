## Ad Creative Notes

**Photos that convert:**
1. True before/after (same angle, same lighting) — roof and house together
2. Driveway being cleaned with visible spray line (half clean, half dirty)
3. Bradley on the job — real person, real equipment
4. Home exterior that looks genuinely different from pre-clean

**What to avoid:**
- Stock photos (kills trust immediately)
- Overly polished images that don't look like NE Ohio homes
- Text overlays heavier than 20% of image area
