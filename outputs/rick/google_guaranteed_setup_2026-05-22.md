## Setup Steps (30–45 Minutes)

### Step 1: Go to the signup page
Visit: **ads.google.com/local-services-ads**
Click "Get started" → select **United States** → **Home Services** → **Cleaning Services / Exterior Cleaning**

### Step 2: Select your services
Check all that apply:
- Exterior house washing
- Roof cleaning
- Driveway and sidewalk cleaning
- Deck and patio cleaning
- Commercial pressure washing

### Step 3: Set your service area
Add by zip code or city. Recommended for Forest City:
- All of Cuyahoga County (Cleveland, Parma, Lakewood, Strongsville, etc.)
- Lake County (Mentor, Willoughby, Painesville)
- Lorain County (Elyria, Avon, North Ridgeville)
- Summit County (Akron, Stow, Fairlawn) — add this even if you're not fully there yet

### Step 4: Complete background check and verification
Google will ask for:
- Business registration (EIN or LLC info)
- Insurance certificate — upload your GL policy PDF
- Personal background check — enter your SSN (Google does a soft pull, not credit)

**Turnaround time: 1–3 business days.** Apply today, you could be live by May 27.

### Step 5: Set your weekly budget
Start with **$200–$300/week ($30–$45/day)**.

At $25/lead average, that's 8–12 leads per week. If you close 30%, that's 2–3 jobs/week from this channel alone at roughly $500–$1,200/job.

**ROI math:** $300/week spend → $1,000–$3,600/week revenue at conservative close rate.

### Step 6: Enable notifications
Set Google to call you immediately when a lead comes in (real-time call forwarding).
- Go to Settings → Notifications → enable "New lead" phone call
