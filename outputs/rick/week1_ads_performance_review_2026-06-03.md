## 15-MINUTE REVIEW PROTOCOL

### Step 1 — Facebook Ads Manager (8 min)

Go to: **business.facebook.com → Ads Manager → your campaigns**

Check these metrics for the period **May 26 – June 2** (7 full days):

| Metric | Good | Okay | Needs attention |
|--------|------|------|-----------------|
| CTR (link click-through rate) | >1.5% | 0.8–1.5% | <0.8% |
| Cost per lead (Lead Gen form) | <$15 | $15–$30 | >$30 |
| Leads generated | 5+ | 2–4 | 0–1 |
| Total spend | ~$210 ($30/day × 7) | — | >$300 (check budget) |
| Frequency | <2.0 | 2.0–2.5 | >2.5 (audience fatigue) |

**What to do based on results:**

- **5+ leads at <$30/CPL:** Increase daily budget by exactly 20% (from $30 → $36). Nothing else.
- **2-4 leads:** Do NOT touch anything. Still in learning phase recovery window. Check again Day 14.
- **0-1 leads:** Check the lead form is working (test submit it yourself). Check the campaign is spending (not paused by Meta). Check lead notification email is active.
- **High frequency (>2.5):** Expand the audience slightly OR add a new ad creative variant (swap the hook text — use `outputs/rick/facebook_ad_creative_voc_refresh_2026-05-24.md` alternatives).

> ⚠️ **DO NOT** restart the campaign, change the objective, or reset audiences — this hard-resets the learning phase and wastes the 7 days already accumulated.

### Step 2 — Google Ads (5 min)

Go to: **ads.google.com → your campaigns → All campaigns**

Check for the period **May 26 – June 2**:

| Metric | Good | Needs attention |
|--------|------|-----------------|
| Impressions | 100+ | <50 (budget or bid too low) |
| Clicks | 10+ | <5 |
| Avg CPC | $2–$5 | >$8 (bid too high; reduce max CPC) |
| Conversions tracked | Any | 0 (check conversion tracking) |
| CTR | >2% | <1% (ad copy or match type issue) |

**What to do:**
- If impressions < 50: increase daily budget by $5–10 or raise max CPC bids by $0.50
- If no conversions tracked: verify Google Tag is firing on thank-you page (check Tag Manager or source code)
- If CTR < 1%: check search terms report — negative keyword additions may be needed

## SCALING DECISION FRAMEWORK (Day 8+)

| Ad results | Decision |
|------------|----------|
| 3+ booked jobs from ads | ✅ Increase budget 20%. Add second creative variant (social proof hook) |
| 1-2 booked jobs, ads spending | ⏳ Hold. Don't change anything. Review again June 10 |
| 0 booked jobs, leads came in | 📞 Call all leads who didn't book. Conversion is a call problem, not ad problem |
| 0 leads, ads spending | 🔧 Check lead form. Check notification email. Test submit the form yourself |
| 0 spend | 🚨 Campaign paused or billing issue. Check billing in Ads Manager immediately |
