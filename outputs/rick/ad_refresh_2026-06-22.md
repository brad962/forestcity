### Ad 1 — Before/After

**Headline (32 chars):** Walk to the End of Your Driveway

**Primary Text:**
"I finally looked at it from the street and I was mortified." That's the moment — when the summer light hits the siding and you see it the way company sees it. The before-and-after photos tell the rest of the story. Book a free quote and see what two hours does.

**CTA Label:** Get a Free Quote

**Creative Direction:**
Two-frame carousel shot from the curb — full front-of-house, same angle before and after. Frame 1: weathered siding with visible algae lines, stained driveway, the kind of shot that makes a homeowner wince. Frame 2: same house, same framing — bright, crisp, looks freshly painted. No text overlays on the main frames. Final card: "Forest City Power Washing · 7 NE Ohio Counties." Best boosted to homeowners 40–60 in Cuyahoga, Lake, and Summit counties.

**Primary Text:**
"The neighbors got theirs done and now ours looks ten times worse." Sound familiar? It's the fastest way to notice your own siding. Forest City Power Washing — insured, we actually show up, and we serve all 7 Northeast Ohio counties. Get your quote before we fill out the week.

**CTA Label:** Get a Free Quote

### Ad 4 — Problem / Solution

**Headline (30 chars):** Sun's Out. Every Streak Shows.

**Primary Text:**
One customer nailed it: "The sun hits it in the morning and you can see every streak." June does that — different angle, higher sun, and suddenly the algae you've been walking past all spring is impossible to ignore. Soft washing kills it at the source. One job, a couple hours, gone.

**CTA Label:** Learn More

**Creative Direction:**
Close-up shot of sun-lit siding — morning golden light raking across horizontal algae streaks, which makes the staining look dramatic and visible. Text overlay lower-left: "You can't unsee it." Second carousel frame: same section of siding, clean and dry, same light. Best format for cold awareness audiences — leads with the problem, earns the click before selling the service. Run against Ad 1 in the Awareness campaign to test which hook performs.
