## Facebook Ad Variations (5)

---

### Ad 1 — Before/After

**Headline (30 chars):** You Won't Recognize Your House

**Primary Text:**
Your neighbors stopped and asked who did it. We cleaned the siding, the driveway, and the roof — and we were done in under two hours. See the before and after.

**CTA Label:** Get a Free Quote

**Creative Direction:**
Side-by-side before/after carousel. Frame 1: green algae-covered siding, dingy driveway. Frame 2: bright, clean exterior, same angle. Bottom caption on Frame 2: "2 hours. Northeast Ohio." No logo until the final frame. Let the transformation do the work.

---

### Ad 2 — Urgency / Seasonal

**Headline (26 chars):** It Turns Green Every June.

**Primary Text:**
Lake Erie humidity hits your siding harder than you think — algae blooms fast once temperatures climb. We're booking June jobs now and filling up. Don't wait until the whole front of your house is green.

**CTA Label:** Book Now

**Creative Direction:**
Single image. Left half: close-up of green/slimy siding. Right half: same wall clean. Text overlay on the clean side: "Book Before We're Full." Orange or red urgency badge in the corner. Summer light, warm tones.

---

### Ad 3 — Social Proof

**Headline (26 chars):** 32 NE Ohio Homes This Week

**Primary Text:**
"My husband thought it was a waste of money. Now he's the one telling everyone about it." — We hear that a lot. Forest City Power Washing is the exterior cleaning crew Northeast Ohio homeowners are booking right now. Get your quote before we're full for the month.

**CTA Label:** Get a Free Quote

**Creative Direction:**
Photo grid layout — 4–6 after shots. Mix of siding, driveway, and roof jobs in recognizable suburb-style homes (ranch, colonial). No text overlay. Clean, real. Small Forest City logo watermark bottom right. Works as a carousel or static mosaic.

---

### Ad 4 — Problem / Solution

**Headline (31 chars):** Black Streaks Aren't Just Ugly.

**Primary Text:**
Those dark lines on your roof are algae — and they eat away at your shingles. A pressure washer pushes them around without killing them. Soft washing kills the algae at the root and doesn't void your warranty.

**CTA Label:** Learn More

**Creative Direction:**
Two-frame carousel or short video. Frame 1: close-up of black roof algae streaks — grim, detailed. Frame 2: same roof section, completely clean. Optional text overlay on Frame 1: "This is alive. It's eating your roof." Bold, not fear-mongering — just honest. Best for retargeting audience.

---

### Ad 5 — Offer

**Headline (31 chars):** Curb Appeal Package — This Week

**Primary Text:**
House washing + driveway cleaning + one extra surface, starting at $299. We're Forest City Power Washing — covering Cuyahoga, Lake, Lorain, and four more counties. Hit the link for a fast quote.

**CTA Label:** Get a Free Quote

**Creative Direction:**
Clean, bright photo of a freshly washed home exterior — summer light, lush lawn, no algae. Small graphic badge in the corner: "Starting at $299." Forest City logo visible. Simple. This is the offer ad, so the photo carries the aspiration and the text closes it.
