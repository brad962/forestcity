## Why This Matters Before Launch

Facebook's algorithm rewards high early engagement. In the first 24–48 hours of a new campaign (the "learning phase"), Facebook needs signals from real users to learn who to show the ads to.

Hooks that use language customers are ALREADY THINKING in their head stop the scroll faster → higher CTR → cheaper cost per lead → better quality score on the algorithm.

The May 20 hooks are good but generic. These hooks use the exact NE Ohio customer language Marcus documented from real reviews.
