## Campaign Structure

**Campaign 1: Awareness — Before/After (Traffic/Engagement)**
- Objective: Reach homeowners in NE Ohio
- Audiences: Cuyahoga + Lake + Lorain + Summit counties, homeowners 35–65, interests: home improvement, real estate, lawn care
- Format: Single image or carousel (before/after)

**Campaign 2: Lead Gen — Quote Request**
- Objective: Lead form completions
- Audience: Same as C1 + Custom audience of page engagers
- Format: Lead ad with built-in form

**Campaign 3: Retargeting — Close the Fence-Sitters**
- Audience: Website visitors (last 60 days) + video viewers (25%+) + page engagers
- Format: Single image + urgency CTA
