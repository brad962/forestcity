## Why Yelp in 2026

Yelp still drives 100+ million unique monthly visitors in the US. In home services categories (cleaning, exterior, power washing), Yelp reviews often rank on the first page of Google search results for queries like "power washing Cleveland reviews." That's search traffic you don't pay for.

Most NE Ohio power washing competitors don't actively manage their Yelp listing. Forest City can own this channel in 15 minutes.

**Cost:** Free (basic listing)
**Time to set up:** 15 minutes
**Ongoing effort:** 5 minutes when a new job is done (request review via text)

---

## Step 1 — Claim the Listing (5 minutes)

1. Go to **biz.yelp.com**
2. Search for "Forest City Power Washing" + Cleveland, OH
3. If it exists: click **"Claim this business"** → verify via phone or email
4. If it doesn't exist: click **"Add your business"** — create from scratch

You'll need to verify you're the owner (automated phone call or email). Takes 2–3 minutes.

---

## Step 3 — Upload Photos (5 minutes, high priority)

Listings with photos get 7x more clicks than listings without.

**Upload these now:**
1. A before/after composite (grab one from outputs/jasmine/ if you have it)
2. A photo of a freshly washed house — bright, clean, curb angle
3. A photo of a clean driveway
4. A photo of a roof soft wash job (if available)
5. Your truck/equipment with Forest City branding

**Photo tips:**
- Natural daylight = best
- Landscape orientation performs better on Yelp
- Label them in the caption: "House wash — Strongsville, OH" or "Roof soft wash — Medina County"

---

## Step 4 — Enable Yelp Request-a-Quote (2 minutes)

1. In your Yelp dashboard → **Business Tools** → **Request a Quote**
2. Turn it ON
3. Set your response time goal: "Within a few hours"

This puts a "Request a Quote" button on your listing. People who want to book without calling will use it.

---

## Step 5 — First Review (do this week)

Your first review matters most. It gets the algorithm moving.

**Who to ask:**
- Any recent customer who said they were happy
- Your best referral partner (a roofer or landscaper you've worked with)
- Your sister company (Fly By Junk Haulers) — cross-review

**How to ask (text script):**
> "Hey [name], quick favor — would you mind leaving Forest City a Yelp review? It takes 30 seconds and really helps. [link] Thanks!"

Get the direct link to your Yelp page from biz.yelp.com under **Profile → Share Profile**.

---

## Ongoing (5 minutes after every job)

After each completed job, send this text:
> "Hey [name] — hope the house is looking great! If you have 30 seconds, a Google or Yelp review goes a long way for us: [link]. Really appreciate it."

Split your review requests between Google and Yelp. Google matters more for search; Yelp builds the Yelp ranking. Alternate: 1 Google → 1 Yelp → 1 Google → etc.

---

## What NOT to Do

- **Don't** ask for reviews in bulk on the same day — Yelp's filter removes them
- **Don't** offer incentives for reviews — Yelp will penalize the listing
- **Don't** respond to negative reviews emotionally — reply professionally, offer to make it right
- **Don't** pay for Yelp ads until you have 10+ organic reviews — the ROI improves dramatically with social proof

## Yelp vs. GBP — What's the Difference

| | Yelp | Google Business Profile |
|---|---|---|
| Review audience | Yelp users | Google search users |
| Search impact | Yelp pages rank on Google | Direct Google rank boost |
| Traffic volume | Lower | Higher |
| Competition | Most competitors ignore it | Most competitors have it |
| Cost | Free basic | Free |

**Use both.** GBP drives the volume. Yelp catches the people who specifically check Yelp before calling.
