## BLOCK 1 — Conversion Tracking ✅ Verify
**Why:** Google Ads will optimize toward whatever it can measure. Without conversion tracking, it optimizes for clicks — not bookings. You will spend money and not know what's converting.

- [ ] Open Google Ads → Tools → Conversions
- [ ] Verify "Calls from ads" conversion action exists + status = Active
- [ ] Verify "Calls from website" conversion action exists + status = Active
- [ ] Confirm the phone number on your ads/website shows the Google forwarding number
- **Guide:** `outputs/rick/google_ads_conversion_tracking_setup_2026-05-25.md`

If not done → STOP and complete conversion tracking setup first. 20 minutes. Non-negotiable.

---

## BLOCK 2 — Payment Method ✅ Verify
**Why:** Google Ads suspends accounts and stops all ads if a payment fails on Day 1.

- [ ] Google Ads → Billing → Payment Methods
- [ ] Confirm a credit card or bank account is attached
- [ ] Confirm no "Billing issue" banner appears at the top of the dashboard
- [ ] Check your daily budget limit: $15–$30/day to start (don't set over $50 until Day 7)

---

## BLOCK 3 — Ad Schedule ✅ Set
**Why:** Running ads at 3am costs money and gets zero calls. Home services ads convert when people are awake and at home.

- [ ] For each campaign: Settings → Ad schedule
- [ ] Set: **Monday–Saturday, 7:00am – 8:00pm**
- [ ] Sunday: can add once you're getting consistent leads (or leave off Week 1)
- [ ] Confirm timezone is set to Eastern Time

---

## BLOCK 4 — Negative Keywords ✅ Add (10-minute task)
**Why:** Without negatives, your ads show for "how to pressure wash yourself" and "power washing jobs hiring." You pay for every click.

**Paste these into each campaign's negative keyword list:**
```
DIY, do it yourself, how to, tutorial, jobs, hiring, employment, career
cheap, free, rent, equipment rental, machine rental
reviews only, yelp reviews
Columbus, Cincinnati, Toledo, Dayton, Youngstown
```

- [ ] Google Ads → Keywords → Negative keywords tab
- [ ] Add at campaign level (applies to all ad groups)
- [ ] Confirm "Power washing jobs" and "how to pressure wash" are blocked

---

## BLOCK 5 — Call Extension ✅ Confirm
**Why:** Call extensions add your phone number directly in the ad. Highest-intent local service leads often click the phone number directly instead of the website. Without it, you lose those calls.

- [ ] Google Ads → Ads → Extensions → Call extensions
- [ ] Verify Forest City phone number is listed
- [ ] Confirm "Enable call reporting" is ON (this is how Google tracks call conversions from ads)
- [ ] Set call reporting hours to match your ad schedule (7am–8pm M–Sat)

## What Success Looks Like on Day 1 (Google Ads)

| Metric | Day 1 Target | If You Don't See It |
|--------|-------------|---------------------|
| Impressions | 50–200 | Bids may be too low — check bid strategy |
| Clicks | 5–20 | CTR < 2% = ad copy not resonating yet (normal Day 1) |
| Calls | 0–3 | Google needs 30–50 clicks before calls start appearing consistently |
| Call duration | >45 sec | Short calls = wrong audience — check negative keywords |
| Quality Score | "—" (forms in 7–10 days) | Normal — don't touch campaigns yet |

**Day 1 rule:** Watch, don't touch. The algorithm needs at least 48–72 hours of data before you adjust anything.

---

## Emergency Stops

**Pause all campaigns if:**
- 20+ clicks, 0 calls, no conversions → negative keyword issue or wrong landing page
- Budget exhausted before 2pm on Day 1 → bid too aggressive, lower to $1.50 CPC Max
- Ads not showing at all after 2 hours → billing issue or campaign still in "Under Review"

**Do NOT pause just because:**
- You only got 2 calls Day 1 (normal)
- Quality Score shows "—" (forms in 7–10 days)
- Cost per click seems high on Day 1 (algorithm hasn't optimized yet)
