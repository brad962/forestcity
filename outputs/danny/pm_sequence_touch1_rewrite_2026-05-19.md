## Touch 1 Rewrite

**CURRENT** — typical of what 0% reply rates look like:
- Subject too long
- Multiple asks in one email
- Reads like a blast, not a person

**REVISED Touch 1**

Subject: `Power washing — [company]?`
*(Replace [company] with the contact's company name. If unknown, use "your properties")*

---

Hey [first name],

I run Forest City Power Washing in Northeast Ohio. We do commercial exterior cleaning — apartment complexes, HOA communities, office parks, retail.

NE Ohio summers are rough on buildings. Between the lake humidity and algae growth, most properties need a full exterior wash at least once a year or complaints start showing up.

Worth a quick 15-min call to see if it's a fit this season?

Bradley
Forest City Power Washing
[phone]

---

**Why this is better:**
- 62 words. Nobody ignores a 62-word email.
- One ask: "worth a quick call?"
- "Lake humidity / algae" is a NE Ohio pain point PMs recognize instantly — it signals you're local, not a blast
- No variables that can fail silently (no {{propertyCount}}, no complex merge fields)
- Subject line looks like a personal email, not a campaign
