## Services

| Service | Method | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| House washing | Soft wash | Removes algae, mold, mildew from siding |
| Roof soft washing | Soft wash only | NEVER pressure wash a roof — voids warranty, causes damage |
| Driveway/concrete cleaning | Pressure wash | Oil stains, tire marks, general grime |
| Deck/fence cleaning | Soft wash or low-pressure | Wood-safe, prep for staining |
| Commercial building washing | Soft or pressure | Storefronts, office buildings, retail |

**Soft wash:** Low-pressure water + biodegradable cleaning solution. Safe for siding, roofs, wood.
**Pressure wash:** High-pressure water. For concrete, brick, hard surfaces only.

## Packages

### The Fresh Start
Entry-level. Single surface (driveway, deck, or siding) or basic whole-home rinse. For customers who want to dip a toe in or have a limited budget. Great upsell starting point.

### The Curb Appeal ⭐ Most Popular
Full exterior package. House washing (all siding surfaces) + driveway cleaning + any one additional surface. Mid-tier pricing. This is the default recommendation for residential homeowners.

### The Showstopper
Premium. Everything in Curb Appeal + roof soft washing + deck or fence cleaning + any detail work (gutters, window frames, etc.). Best for homes going on market, after major weather events, or homeowners who want everything perfect.

### Annual Maintenance Plan
Two visits: spring clean (March–April) + pre-winter clean (October–November). Discounted vs. booking twice separately. Best pitch for existing customers. Recurring revenue.

## Top 3 Objections (and how to handle them)

**1. "I can get it cheaper on Facebook."**
Response: "You can, and some of those guys are great. But a lot of them don't show up, don't have insurance, and you don't know what chemicals they're putting on your siding. We're not the cheapest — we're the ones who show up, do it right, and stand behind it. Call us if the other guy doesn't show."

**2. "I'll just rent a pressure washer."**
Response: "For concrete, totally makes sense. But for your siding or roof, a pressure washer can drive water behind the panels and cause rot, or strip the granules off your shingles and void your warranty. Soft washing is the only safe method for those surfaces — and it actually kills the algae instead of just knocking it around."

**3. "I don't need it that often."**
Response: "In Northeast Ohio, with the lake humidity and the weather we get, algae and mold come back in 12–18 months. Once you see the black streaks on your roof, you're already at risk of shingle damage. Most of our customers do it every 1–2 years. The annual plan locks in the discount and you don't have to think about it."

## Brand Voice

**Sound like Bradley:** direct, friendly, Cleveland-local. He's a real guy who knows his craft and takes pride in the work. He doesn't use corporate language. He talks to customers like a neighbor, not a salesman.

**Do:**
- Use contractions
- Reference Northeast Ohio, specific counties, local landmarks
- Short sentences. Active voice.
- Lead with the customer's problem, not our features

**Don't:**
- "Leverage," "synergize," "world-class," "best-in-class"
- Passive voice
- Generic "we are committed to excellence" filler
- Anything that sounds like it came from a marketing agency
