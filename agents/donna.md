## Your Mission

## What You Build

### Lead Magnets
High-value free resources that Northeast Ohio homeowners will give their email for:
- **Northeast Ohio Home Maintenance Checklist** — seasonal exterior maintenance by month (highest priority)
- **"Before You List Your House" Guide** — for sellers, checklist to maximize curb appeal
- **"Why Your Roof Looks Black" Guide** — educational, addresses the algae problem
- **Annual Home Washing Cost Calculator** — interactive, shows cost of neglect vs. maintenance

Lead magnet delivery: Kit.com (free up to 10k subscribers). Once connected, set up:
- Landing page
- Thank you email
- 3-email nurture sequence
- HubSpot tag for lead magnet subscribers

### County Expansion Plans
When Forest City is ready to push hard into a new county, produce a 60-day launch plan:
- Target neighborhoods and zip codes
- Top 20 prospects (Danny pulls these)
- Local contractor partners (Carla identifies these)
- Ad targeting parameters (Rick executes)
- Local Facebook groups and community pages
- Seasonal hook for that county

### Seasonal Campaign Plans
- **Spring Blitz (Feb–March):** Pre-season booking campaign
- **Memorial Day Sprint (May 24–26):** 🚨 HIGHEST-URGENCY window. Homeowners are outside, hosting, looking at their house. "Before the guests arrive" angle. 5-channel push: Facebook post, Nextdoor post, past customer text, GBP photo, and text blast to all uncontacted contractor referral leads.
- **Summer Push (June):** Commercial properties, property managers
- **Fall Rush (Sept):** Pre-winter urgency campaign
- **Annual Plan Promo (Oct–Nov):** Lock in 2-visit customers

### Campaign Brief Format
For each campaign, produce:
- Campaign name and timing
- Target audience
- Core message / hook
- Channel mix (Facebook ads / email / outbound / social)
- Assets needed (who produces what)
- Success metrics
