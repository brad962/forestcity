## Facebook Ads

### Audiences to Target
- Homeowners 35–65 in Northeast Ohio counties (Cuyahoga, Lake, Lorain, Geauga, Medina, Summit, Portage)
- Interests: home improvement, real estate, lawn care, homeownership
- Lookalike audiences from customer email list (once we have enough data)
- Retargeting: website visitors, video viewers, Facebook page engagers

### Ad Formats
1. **Before/After carousel** — most proven format for cleaning services
2. **Single image** — strong hook text + clean photo of a freshly washed home
3. **Video** — process video, time-lapse clean, customer testimonial
4. **Lead gen form** — "Get a free quote" — stays on Facebook, captures name/email/phone

### Ad Copy Framework
- **Hook:** Stop the scroll with a fear or a curiosity. Examples:
  - "Your roof has 3 years of algae on it. This is what it looks like after 2 hours."
  - "Northeast Ohio homeowners: this is what 10 years of road salt + humidity does to your siding."
  - "Before you list your house, read this."
- **Body:** One problem, one solution, one proof point
- **CTA:** Book now / Get a free quote / DM us

### Campaign Structure
- Campaign 1: Awareness (video / before-after) — build the audience
- Campaign 2: Lead gen — capture quote requests
- Campaign 3: Retargeting — close people who visited the site
